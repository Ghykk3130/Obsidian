# 1. $\ket{\theta,\phi}$的引入·

>[!Note] Definition 1
>Define $\ket{r,\theta,\phi}$ as:
>$$\ket{r,\theta,\phi} =\ket{x=r\sin \theta \cos \phi,y=r\sin \theta \sin \phi,z=r\cos \phi} $$

这样定义的话，我们必存在normalization：

>[!Note] Proposition 1
>The normalization of $\ket{r,\theta,\phi}$ is:
>$$\int_{0}^{\infty}dr\int_{0}^{\pi}d\theta \int_{0}^{2\pi}d\phi r^{2}\sin \theta \bra{r,\theta,\phi} r,\theta,\phi\rangle=1$$

^bac134

## Proof.
我们知道：
$$\begin{align}
 & \int d^{3}r\bra{x=r\sin \theta \cos \phi,y=r\sin \theta \sin \phi,z=r\cos \phi}x=r\sin \theta \cos \phi,y=r\sin \theta \sin \phi,z=r\cos \phi\rangle=1 \\
\implies & \int d^{3}r\bra{r,\theta,\phi} r,\theta,\phi\rangle=1 
\end{align}$$
将测度换元则有：
$$d^{3}r=r^{2}\sin \theta d{\theta}d\phi$$
Then done.
>[!Right]
>$\blacksquare$

然后我们定义：

>[!Note] Definition 2
>Define $\ket{\theta,\phi}$ as:
>$$\ket{\theta,\phi} =\int_{0}^{\infty}drr^{2}\ket{r,\theta,\phi} $$

>[!Note] Corollary 1
>The normalization of $\ket{\theta,\phi}$ is:
>$$\int_{0}^{\pi}d\theta \int_{0}^{2\pi}d\phi \sin \theta \bra{\theta,\phi} \theta,\phi\rangle=1$$
## Proof.
Trivial by [[球谐函数与旋转表示#^bac134|proposition 1.1]].
>[!Right]
>$\blacksquare$
# 2. 球谐函数

我们知道角动量算符$L^{2},L_{z}$可以在坐标表象下展开。即：
$$\vec{L}=\vec{r}\times \vec{p}=\vec{r}\times(-i\hbar \nabla)$$
容易计算，在经过换元：
$$\begin{align}
 & x=r\sin \theta \cos \phi \\
 & y=r\sin \theta \sin \phi \\
 & z=r\cos \phi
\end{align}$$
之后，我们在坐标表象下有：
$$\begin{align}
 & L^{2}=-\hbar^{2}\left(  \frac{1}{\sin \theta} \frac{\partial}{\partial \theta}\left( \sin \theta \frac{\partial}{\partial \theta} \right)+ \frac{1}{\sin ^{2}\theta} \frac{\partial^{2}}{\partial \phi^{2}} \right) \\
  & L_{z}=-i\hbar \frac{\partial}{\partial \phi}
\end{align}$$
