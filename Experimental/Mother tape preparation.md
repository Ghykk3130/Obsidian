## Step 1
- Clean off the table. Avoid SiO2 pieces.
- Cut a long piece of single-sided white tape, stick the middle and top of the white tape to table with single sided white tape.
## Step 2
- Open the bulk container. The lid of the container should be facing down the table to avoid dust. The lid should not be open for more than 1 min.
- Clean off the tweezer with acetone directly, then wipe off acetone using the paper in the overhead drawer. Then change gloves.
- Pick one piece of crystal, lie it on the top part of the tape. 
- Put the bottom part of the tape gently over the top part, partially overlapped to exfoliate.
- First create a line, and then the entire pattern.
## Step 3
- Razor blade to cut the mother tape.
- Fold the sticky part of the mother tape.
- Put double-sided tapes inside the container, and put the mother tape on it.
- Use single sided tape to seal the container.
- Label with: material type+name+date
