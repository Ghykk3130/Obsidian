The presentation is very illuminating, providing the audiences with an intuitive introduction to Hubbard model. The experimental data supporting robust superconductivity in $3.5^\circ$ and $3.65^\circ$ $tWSe_2$ is compelling. With minor additions regarding the specific pairing symmetry and the scaling of Hubbard parameters in moiré lattices, this presentation provides a very clear overview of the current state of the field. The part I favor most is the intuition for Mott insulator, based on energy argument. One thing that might be helpful is to provide more formulas and toy models for the audiences to understand.
