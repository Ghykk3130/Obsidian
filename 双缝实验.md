考虑粒子从某个furnace的出口出来。令furnace的出口为$\mathbb{R}^{3}$中的区域$\Sigma$。这implicit地测量了粒子的位置。所以粒子坍缩到符合$\Sigma$限制的位置本征态的叠加上。即：
$$\psi(\vec{r},0)\text{ suppoted on }\Sigma$$
这即是$0$时刻，funace出口出来的粒子的态。

假设双缝实验中，遮住粒子的部分在空间中占据区域$\gamma$。那么整个体系的势能为：
$$V(\vec{r})= \left\{\begin{align}
 & 0,\vec{r} \not\in \gamma \\
 & \infty,\vec{r}\in \gamma
\end{align}\right.$$
考虑时间演化算子对于波函数的作用：
$$\psi(\vec{r},t)=\bra{\vec{r}} \exp\left( - \frac{i}{\hbar}\left(  \frac{p^{2}}{2m}+V(\vec{r}) \right)t \right)\ket{\psi,0} $$
我们需要计算传播子：
$$K(\vec{r},t,\vec{r}^{'},0)=\bra{\vec{r}} \exp\left( - \frac{i}{\hbar}\left(  \frac{p^{2}}{2m}+V(\vec{r}) \right)t \right)\ket{\vec{r}^{'}}=\int_{\text{all paths }\Gamma \text{ connecting }\vec{r},\vec{r}^{'}}\mathscr{D}(\Gamma)e^{\frac{i}{\hbar}S(\Gamma)}  $$
考虑如下定理：

>[!Note] Theorem 1
>$$\lim_{ \lambda \to \pm\infty } \int_{a}^b dx f(x)e^{i\lambda g(x)}=0$$

那么，

>[!Note] Proposition 1
>The paths in the path integral can be reduced to the set of paths that pass through the double slits.

^1e8828

## Proof.
若$\Gamma$穿过$\gamma$，则考虑一个$\Gamma$的分割$\vec{r}_{0},\dots,\vec{r}_{N-1}$。则对于足够精细的分割，必有：
$$S(\text{partition})\approx S(\Gamma) =\int_{0}^tdtL=-\infty$$
那么：
$$\int_{\Gamma}\mathscr{D}(\Gamma)e^{\frac{i}{\hbar}S(\Gamma)}=\lim_{ N \to \infty } \left( \frac{e^{- \frac{\pi}{2}i}m}{2\pi \hbar\delta t} \right)^{3N/2} \int d^{3}r_{0}\dots  d^{3}r_{N-1}e^{\frac{i}{\hbar}S(\text{partition})}=0$$
于是路径积分只能取穿过双缝的路径。即：
$$K(\vec{r},t,\vec{r}^{'},0)=\int_{\Gamma \text{ passing through }\gamma^c}\mathscr{D}(\Gamma)e^{\frac{i}{\hbar}S(\Gamma)}$$
>[!Right]
>$\blacksquare$

为了将传播子写为更简单形式，我们证明传播子的复合形式：

>[!Note] Proposition 2
>$$K(\vec{r},t,\vec{r}^{'},t^{'})=\int d^{3}r^{''}K(\vec{r},t,\vec{r}^{''},t^{''})K(\vec{r}^{''},t^{''},\vec{r}^{'},t^{'})$$
## Proof.
$$\begin{align}
K(\vec{r},t,\vec{r}^{'},t^{'}) & = \bra{\vec{r}} \mathscr{U}(t,t^{'})\ket{\vec{r}^{'}}  \\
 & = \bra{\vec{r}} \mathscr{U}(t,t^{''})\mathscr{U}(t^{''},t^{'})\ket{\vec{r}^{'}}  \\
 & = \int d^{3}r^{''}\bra{\vec{r}} \mathscr{U}(t,t^{''})\ket{\vec{r}^{''}} \bra{\vec{r}^{''}} \mathscr{U}(t^{''},t^{'})\ket{\vec{r}^{'}}  \\
  & =\int d^{3}r^{''}K(\vec{r},t,\vec{r}^{''},t^{''})K(\vec{r}^{''},t^{''},\vec{t}^{'},t^{'})
\end{align}$$
>[!Right]
>$\blacksquare$

那么，取$\vec{r}^{''}$在双缝平面上，我们有：
$$K(\vec{r},t,\vec{r}^{'},t^{'})=\int d^{3}r^{''}K(\vec{r},t,\vec{r}^{''},t^{''})K(\vec{r}^{''},t^{''},\vec{r}^{'},t^{'})$$
>[!Note] Proposition 3
>$$K(\vec{r},t,\vec{r}^{'},t^{'})=\int_{\gamma^c} d^{3}r^{''}K(\vec{r},t,\vec{r}^{''},t^{''})K(\vec{r}^{''},t^{''},\vec{r}^{'},t^{'})$$
## Proof.
若$\vec{r}^{''}\in \gamma$，且将双缝平面上的路径起点算进计算$K(\vec{r},t,\vec{r}^{''},t^{''})$的路径积分积分，那么$K(\vec{r},t,\vec{r}^{''},t^{''})=0$。这是因为由于[[双缝实验#^1e8828|proposition 1]], 所有从$\vec{r}^{''}$到$\vec{r}$路径都不会穿过双缝，所以路径积分取不了任何路径，为零。因此只能取$\vec{r}^{''}$穿过双缝的情形。故：
$$K(\vec{r},t,\vec{r}^{'},t^{'})=\int_{\gamma^c} d^{3}r^{''}K(\vec{r},t,\vec{r}^{''},t^{''})K(\vec{r}^{''},t^{''},\vec{r}^{'},t^{'})$$
>[!Right]
>$\blacksquare$
