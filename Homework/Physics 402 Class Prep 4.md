If the wave is travelling to the negative x direction, then I can just displace the wave form $y=\frac{4m^{3}}{x^{2}+2m^{2}}$ to the left by $vt$. Then I have:
$$y(x,t)=\frac{4m^{3}}{(x+vt)^{2}+2m^{2}}=\frac{4m^{3}}{(x+2.5t)^{2}+2m^{2}}$$
