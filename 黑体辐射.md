>In a canonical ensemble, the number of photons can change.

那么我们可以得到推论：

>[!Note] Proposition 1
>For photon gas at equilibrium, we have that $\mu=0$.
## Proof.
考虑一正则系综。在平衡位置附近作绕动：
$$dF=-pdV+\mu dN=0$$
其中$-SdT=0$是因为是正则系综。$\mu dN$没写等于零是因为[[黑体辐射#^dbea59|postulate 1]]。因为$dN\neq 0$，所以$\mu=0$。
>[!Right]
>$\blacksquare$

>[!Note] Proposition 2 (Stefan-Boltzmann law)
>The energy density of photon gas is $u=\frac{\pi^{2}k^4}{15\hbar^{3}c^{3} }T^{4}$
## Proof.
我们计算能量：
$$\begin{align}
U & = \frac{V}{(2\pi)^{3}}2\int d^{3}k \frac{\epsilon}{e^{\beta\epsilon}-1}
\end{align}$$
其中$2$是因为有两种偏振。

我们试图将其转化成能量的测度。我们有$\epsilon=\hbar \omega=\hbar ck$。所以：
$$\begin{align}
\frac{V}{(2\pi)^{3}}2\int d^{3}k & = \frac{V}{(2\pi)^{3}}2\int_{0}^{\infty}  dk 4\pi k^{2} \\
 & = \frac{8V\pi^{2}}{(hc)^{3}}\int_{0}^{\infty}d\epsilon \epsilon^{2}
\end{align}$$
所以：
$$\begin{align}
U & = \frac{8V\pi^{2}}{(hc)^{3}}\int_{0}^{\infty}d\epsilon \frac{\epsilon^{3}}{e^{\beta\epsilon}-1} \\
 & = \frac{8V\pi^{2}}{(hc)^{3}}(kT)^4g_{4}(1) \Gamma(4)\\
 & = \frac{48V\pi^{2}}{(hc)^{3}}\zeta(4)k^4T^4 \\
 & = \frac{\pi^{2}k^4}{15\hbar^{3}c^{3} }VT^{4}
\end{align}$$
>[!Right]
>$\blacksquare$
## Remark
这个结果和一般地Bose gas不同，因为$\epsilon$的k-dependence不一样。

>[!Note] Proposition 3 (Planck's law)
>The distribution of $u$ with respect to $\omega$ is $\frac{du}{d\omega}=\frac{\hbar }{c^{3}\pi^{2}} \frac{1}{e^{\beta \hbar \omega}-1}\omega^{3}$
## Proof.
我们有：
$$\begin{align}
U & = \frac{V}{(2\pi)^{3}}2\int d^{3}k \frac{\epsilon}{e^{\beta\epsilon}-1}
\end{align}$$
我们试图将其转化成$\omega$的测度。我们有$\epsilon=\hbar \omega=\hbar ck$。所以：
$$\begin{align}
\frac{V}{(2\pi)^{3}}2\int d^{3}k & = \frac{V}{(2\pi)^{3}}2\int_{0}^{\infty}  dk 4\pi k^{2} \\
 & = \frac{V}{\pi^{2}c^{3}} \int d\omega \omega^{2}
\end{align}$$
所以：
$$\begin{align}
 & U= \frac{V}{\pi^{2}c^{3}} \int d\omega \omega^{2} \frac{\epsilon}{e^{\beta\epsilon}-1} \\
\implies &  \frac{du}{d\omega}= \frac{1}{c^{3}\pi^{2}} \omega^{2} \frac{\epsilon}{e^{\beta\epsilon}-1}= \frac{\hbar }{c^{3}\pi^{2}} \frac{1}{e^{\beta \hbar \omega}-1}\omega^{3}
\end{align}$$


## Caveat

为了从$U$得到$p$，我们不能直接用$p= - \left( \frac{\partial U}{\partial V} \right)_{S,N}$。这是因为对于光子气，不可能fix $N$。
