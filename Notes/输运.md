# 1. Boltzmann输运方程

设非平衡态占据数为$f(\mathbf{r},\mathbf{k},t)$。追随着半经典态$(\mathbf{r},\mathbf{k})$，一方面我们有：
$$\frac{Df}{Dt}= \frac{\partial f}{\partial t}+ \frac{\partial f}{\partial \mathbf{r}}\cdot  \dot{\mathbf{r}}+ \frac{\partial f}{\partial \mathbf{k}}\cdot  \dot{\mathbf{k}}$$
另一方面，电子可以经过碰撞进入态$(\mathbf{r},\mathbf{k})$，也可由于recombination进入态$(\mathbf{r},\mathbf{k})$。所以：
$$\frac{Df}{Dt}=\left( \frac{\partial f}{\partial t} \right)_{\text{coll}}+ \left( \frac{\partial f}{\partial t} \right)_{\text{rec}}$$
我们得到Boltzmann输运方程：
$$\left( \frac{\partial f}{\partial t} \right)_{\text{coll}}+\left( \frac{\partial f}{\partial t} \right)_{\text{rec}}= \frac{\partial f}{\partial t}+ \frac{\partial f}{\partial \mathbf{r}}\cdot   \dot{\mathbf{r}}+ \frac{\partial f}{\partial \mathbf{k}}\cdot  \dot{\mathbf{k}}$$
一般来说，假设recombination不发生，$(\partial f /\partial t)_{\text{rec}}=0$。根据relaxation time approximation，可以认为$(\partial f /\partial t)_{\text{coll}}=- \frac{f-f^{0}}{\tau}=- \frac{g}{\tau}$。则可以重写Boltzmann输运方程：

>[!Success] Proposition 1.1
>$$- \frac{g}{\tau}= \frac{\partial f}{\partial t}+ \frac{\partial f}{\partial \mathbf{r}}\cdot  \dot{\mathbf{r}}+ \frac{\partial f}{\partial \mathbf{k}}\cdot  \dot{\mathbf{k}}$$

在输运计算中，我们常常将方程线性化。即：
$$\frac{\partial f}{\partial \mathbf{r}}= \frac{\partial f}{\partial T} \frac{\partial T}{\partial \mathbf{r}}\approx \frac{\partial f^{(0)}}{\partial T} \frac{\partial T}{\partial \mathbf{r}},\ \frac{\partial f}{\partial \mathbf{k}}= \frac{\partial f}{\partial\epsilon} \frac{\partial\epsilon}{\partial \mathbf{k}}\approx \frac{\partial f^{(0)}}{\partial\epsilon} \frac{\partial\epsilon}{\partial \mathbf{k}}$$
那么方程化为：
$$- \frac{g}{\tau}= \frac{\partial f}{\partial t}+ \frac{\partial f^{(0)}}{\partial T} \frac{\partial T}{\partial \mathbf{r}}\cdot  \dot{\mathbf{r}}+ \frac{\partial f^{(0)}}{\partial\epsilon} \frac{\partial\epsilon}{\partial \mathbf{k}}\cdot  \dot{\mathbf{k}}$$

# 2. 输运系数

>[!Success] Proposition 2.1
>$$\mathbf{j}_{q}= \mathbf{j}_{u}-\mu \mathbf{j}_{N}$$
## Proof.

假设热豫驰时间极短，以至于任意一个体积随时处于热平衡之中。考虑一个体积$\Sigma$。则：
$$\begin{align}
 & dU=dQ-\mu dN \\
\implies &  \int_{\Sigma}d^{2}r  \hat{\mathbf{n}}\cdot \mathbf{j}_{u} = \int_{\Sigma}d^{2}r  \hat{\mathbf{n}}\cdot \mathbf{j}_{q} - \mu \int_{\Sigma}d^{2}r  \hat{\mathbf{n}} \cdot  \mathbf{j}_{N}
\end{align}$$
由于$\Sigma$的任意性脱去积分即可。
>[!Right]
>$\blacksquare$
