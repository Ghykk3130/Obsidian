考虑固体当中存在形变映射$\mathbf{R}(\mathbf{r})$。定义固体形变场：
$$\mathbf{u}(\mathbf{r})=\mathbf{R}(\mathbf{r})-\mathbf{r}$$
那么对于固体中相隔$d\mathbf{r}$的两点，我们可以求得度规变化：
$$\begin{align}
(d\mathbf{R})^{2}-(d\mathbf{r})^{2} &  = (d\mathbf{r}+d\mathbf{u})^{2}-(d\mathbf{r})^{2} \\
 & = dr_{i}^{2}+ du_{i}^{2}+ 2dr_{i}du_{i}-dr_{i}^{2} \\
 & = du_{i}^{2}+2dr_{i}du_{i} \\
 & = \frac{\partial u_{i}}{\partial r_{j}}dr_{j} \frac{\partial u_{i}}{\partial r_{k}}dr_{k}+ 2 \frac{\partial u_{i}}{\partial r_{j}}dr_{i}dr_{j} \\
 & = \frac{\partial u_{i}}{\partial r_{j}} \frac{\partial u_{i}}{\partial r_{k}}dr_{j}dr_{k}+ 2\frac{\partial u_{j}}{\partial r_{k} }dr_{j}dr_{k} \\
 & = \frac{\partial u_{i}}{\partial r_{j}} \frac{\partial u_{i}}{\partial r_{k}}dr_{j}dr_{k}+ \frac{\partial u_{j}}{\partial r_{k}}dr_{j}dr_{k}+ \frac{\partial u_{k}}{\partial r_{j} }dr_{k}dr_{j} \\
 & = 2\cdot \frac{1}{2}\left(  \frac{\partial u_{j}}{\partial r_{k}}+ \frac{\partial u_{k}}{\partial r_{j}}+ \frac{\partial u_{i}}{\partial r_{j}}  \frac{\partial u_{i}}{\partial r_{k}} \right)dr_{j}dr_{k}
\end{align}$$
所以可以定义strain tensor：
$$u_{jk}= \frac{1}{2}\left(\frac{\partial u_{j}}{\partial r_{k}}+\frac{\partial u_{k}}{\partial r_{j}}+ \frac{\partial u_{i}}{\partial r_{j}} \frac{\partial u_{i}}{\partial r_{k}}\right)$$

注意到：
$$\begin{align}
\frac{\partial R_{i}}{\partial r_{j}} & = \frac{\partial}{\partial r_{j}}(u_{i}+r_{i}) \\
 & = \frac{\partial u_{i} }{\partial r_{j} }+\delta_{ij}
\end{align}$$
所以：
$$\begin{align}
\Lambda_{ij}\Lambda_{ik} & = \frac{\partial u_{i}}{\partial r_{j}} \frac{\partial u_{i}}{\partial r_{k}}+ \frac{\partial u_{k}}{\partial r_{j}}+ \frac{\partial u_{j}}{\partial r_{k}}+\delta_{ij}\delta_{ik} \\
 & = \frac{\partial u_{i}}{\partial r_{j}} \frac{\partial u_{i}}{\partial r_{k}}+ \frac{\partial u_{k}}{\partial r_{j}}+ \frac{\partial u_{j}}{\partial r_{k}}+ \delta_{jk}
\end{align}$$
Then done.

(dR)^{2} =|\mathbf{R}(\mathbf{r}+d\mathbf{r})-\mathbf{R}(\mathbf{r}) |^{2} & = \left(\frac{\partial \mathbf{R}}{\partial \mathbf{r}}\cdot d\mathbf{r}\right)\cdot\left( \frac{\partial \mathbf{R}}{\partial \mathbf{r}}\cdot d\mathbf{r} \right) \\

\end{align}$$

Strain tensor是$\{ \mathbf{r} \}$上的张量场。所以$\boldsymbol{\epsilon}=\boldsymbol{\epsilon}(\mathbf{r})$。我们假设弹性能密度是strain tensor的泛函，即$u=u(\boldsymbol{\epsilon})$。我们可以定义stress tensor：

>[!Note] Definition 2
>Define the stress tensor:
>$$T_{ij}= \frac{\partial u}{\partial\epsilon_{ij}}$$

Stress tensor和strain tensor大约互为广义力和广义坐标。我们还可以定义弹性张量，来characterize $\mathbf{T}$对于$\boldsymbol{\epsilon}$的响应：

>[!Note] Definition 3
>Define the elasticity tensor $C_{ijkl}$ by:
>$$T_{ij}=C_{ijkl}\epsilon_{kl}$$

## Ex:

所以若$\mathbf{T}$，$\boldsymbol{\epsilon}$呈线性关系，我们有：
$$C_{ijkl}= \frac{T_{ij}}{\epsilon_{kl}}= \frac{\partial T_{ij}}{\partial\epsilon_{kl}}= \frac{\partial^{2}u}{\partial\epsilon_{ij}\partial\epsilon_{kl}}$$
在平衡位置附近展开能量密度得到：
$$u(\boldsymbol{\epsilon}_{0}+d\boldsymbol{\epsilon})\approx u(\boldsymbol{\epsilon}_{0})+ \frac{\partial u}{\partial \epsilon_{ij}} d\epsilon_{ij}+ \frac{1}{2} \frac{\partial^{2}u}{\partial\epsilon_{ij}\partial\epsilon_{kl}}d\epsilon_{ij}d\epsilon_{kl}= u(\epsilon_{0})+ \frac{1}{2} \frac{\partial^{2}u}{\partial\epsilon_{ij}\partial\epsilon_{kl}}d\epsilon_{ij}d\epsilon_{kl}= u(\boldsymbol{\epsilon}_{0})+ \frac{1}{2}C_{ijkl}d\epsilon_{ij}d\epsilon_{kl}$$
此后，我们普遍假设stress和strain呈线性关系。

>[!Success] Proposition 1
>$C_{ijkl}$ only has 21 independent components.
## Proof.

首先，因为$T_{ij},\ \epsilon_{ij}$都是对称张量，所以：
$$C_{ijkl}= \frac{T_{ij}}{\epsilon_{kl}}= \frac{T_{ji}}{\epsilon_{lk}}=C_{jilk}$$
所以$i,j$指标中的$3 \times 3 =9$个自由度只有$6$个是自由的。$k,l$同理。原先$\mathbf{C} \in \mathbb{R}^{3}\otimes \mathbb{R}^{3}\otimes \mathbb{R}^{3}\otimes \mathbb{R}^{3}$。现在前面的$\mathbb{R}^{3}\otimes \mathbb{R}^{3}$的九个独立基底被削减为六个，即$\text{Sym}(\mathbb{R}^{3}\otimes \mathbb{R}^{3})$的基底$e_{1} e_{1}, e_{2}e_{2},e_{3}e_{3}, \frac{1}{2}(e_{1}e_{2}+e_{2}e_{1}), \frac{1}{2}(e_{1}e_{3}+e_{3}e_{1}), \frac{1}{2}(e_{2}e_{3}+e_{3}e_{2})$。后面的$\mathbb{R}^{3}\otimes \mathbb{R}^{3}$同理。那么现在两个空间再作张量积独立基底只有$6 \times 6=36$个。

又注意到：
$$\begin{align}
 & \frac{\partial^{2} u}{\partial\epsilon_{ij}\partial\epsilon_{kl}}= \frac{\partial^{2}u }{\partial\epsilon_{kl}\partial\epsilon_{ij} }\implies C_{ijkl}=C_{klij}
\end{align}$$
所以任意交换前后两个$\text{Sym}(\mathbb{R}^{3}\otimes \mathbb{R}^{3})$的基底又是没有影响的。空间进一步缩减为$\text{Sym}(\text{Sym}(\mathbb{R}^{3}\otimes \mathbb{R}^{3})\otimes\text{Sym}(\mathbb{R}^{3}\otimes \mathbb{R}^{3}))$。我们只用求$6\times 6$对称矩阵的独立变量个数。为对角线及以上的$21$个。
>[!Right]
>$\blacksquare$
