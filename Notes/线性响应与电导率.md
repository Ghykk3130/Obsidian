# 1. 电流密度的二次量子化

考虑无自旋相互作用电子气的hamiltonian：
$$\begin{align}
H & = \int d^{d}x\psi ^{\dagger}(x)\left( - \frac{1}{2m}\nabla^{2} +U(x)\right)\psi(x)+ \frac{1}{2}\int d^{d}x d^{d}y \psi ^{\dagger}(x)\psi ^{\dagger}(y)V(x-y)\psi(y)\psi(x)
\end{align}$$
切换到Heisenberg绘景，我们通过连续性方程$\frac{\partial \rho}{\partial t}=-\nabla \cdot \mathbf{j}$得到粒子流密度。而：
$$\begin{align}
\frac{\partial \rho}{\partial t} & = \frac{\partial}{\partial t}(\psi ^{\dagger}\psi ) \\
 & = \frac{\partial \psi ^{\dagger}}{\partial t}\psi+ \psi ^{\dagger} \frac{\partial \psi}{\partial t}
\end{align}$$
其中，场算符的时间导数由Heisenberg运动方程给出。令$H_{0}= \int d^{d}x \psi ^{\dagger}\left( - \frac{1}{2m}\nabla^{2}+U \right)\psi$，我们有：
$$\begin{align}
[\psi ^{\dagger}(x^{'}),H_{0}] & = \int d^{d}x \psi ^{\dagger}(x^{'})\psi ^{\dagger}(x)\left( - \frac{1}{2m}\nabla_{x}^{2}+U \right)\psi(x)- \int d^{d}x \psi ^{\dagger}(x)\left( - \frac{1}{2m}\nabla^{2}+U \right)\psi(x)\psi ^{\dagger}(x^{'}) \\
 & = -\int d^{d}x \psi ^{\dagger}(x)\left( - \frac{1}{2m}\nabla^{2}+U \right)\psi ^{\dagger}(x^{'})\psi(x)- \int d^{d}x \psi ^{\dagger}(x)\left( - \frac{1}{2m}\nabla^{2}+U \right)\psi(x)\psi ^{\dagger}(x^{'})  \\
 & = -\int d^{d}x \psi ^{\dagger}(x)\left( - \frac{1}{2m}\nabla^{2}+{U} \right)[\delta(x^{'}-x)-\psi(x)\psi ^{\dagger}(x^{'})]-\int d^{d}x \psi ^{\dagger}(x)\left( - \frac{1}{2m}\nabla^{2}+U \right)\psi(x)\psi ^{\dagger}(x^{'})  \\
 & = -\int d^{d}x \psi ^{\dagger}(x)\left( - \frac{1}{2m}\nabla^{2}+U  \right)\delta(x^{'}-x)
\end{align}$$
我们先证明一条引理：

>[!Success] Lemma 1.1
>$\int d^{d}xf(x)\nabla_{x}^{2}\delta(x-x^{'})= \nabla_{x^{'}}^{2}f$
## Proof.

我们先证明一维，然后直接推广到n维。考虑：
$$\begin{align}
\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}dx f(x) \frac{\partial^{2}}{\partial x^{2}}\delta(x-x^{'}) & = \left[ f(x) \frac{\partial}{\partial x}\delta(x-x^{'}) \right]_{-\infty}^{\infty}- \int dx \frac{\partial f}{\partial x} \frac{\partial}{\partial x}\delta(x-x^{'}) \\
 & = -\left[  \frac{\partial f}{\partial x}\delta(x-x^{'}) \right]_{-\infty}^{\infty}+ \int dx \frac{\partial^{2}f}{\partial x^{2}}\delta(x-x^{'}) \\
 & = f^{''}(x^{'})
\end{align}$$
>[!Right]
>$\blacksquare$

于是：
$$\begin{align}
[\psi ^{\dagger}(x^{'}),H_{0}] & = -\left( - \frac{1}{2m}\nabla^{2}_{x^{'}}+U(x^{'}) \right)\psi ^{\dagger}(x^{'})
\end{align}$$
令相互作用项为$H_{\text{int}}= \frac{1}{2}\int d^{d}x d^{d}y \psi ^{\dagger}(x)\psi ^{\dagger}(y)V(x-y)\psi(y)\psi(x)$。那么：
$$\begin{align}
[\psi ^{\dagger}(x^{'}),H_{\text{int}}] & = \frac{1}{2}\int d^{d}xd^{d}y \psi ^{\dagger}(x^{'})\psi ^{\dagger}(x)\psi ^{\dagger}(y)V(x-y)\psi(y)\psi(x)- \frac{1}{2}\int d^{d}xd^{d}y \psi ^{\dagger}(x)\psi ^{\dagger}(y)V(x-y)\psi(y)\psi(x)\psi ^{\dagger}(x^{'}) \\
 & = \frac{1}{2}\int d^{d}x d^{d}y\psi ^{\dagger}(x)\psi ^{\dagger}(y)V(x-y)\delta(x^{'}-y)\psi(x)- \frac{1}{2}\int d^{d}x d^{d}y \psi ^{\dagger}(x)\psi ^{\dagger}(y)V(x-y)\psi(y)\psi ^{\dagger}(x^{'})\psi(x)- \frac{1}{2}\int d^{d}xd^{d}y \psi ^{\dagger}(x)\psi ^{\dagger}(y)V(x-y)\psi(y)\psi(x)\psi ^{\dagger}(x^{'})  \\
 & = \frac{1}{2}\int d^{d}x d^{d}y\psi ^{\dagger}(x)\psi ^{\dagger}(y)V(x-y)\delta(x^{'}-y)\psi(x)- \frac{1}{2}\int d^{d}xd^{d}y \psi ^{\dagger}(x)\psi ^{\dagger}(y)V(x-y)\psi(y)\delta(x-x^{'}) \\
 & = \int d^{d}x \psi ^{\dagger}(x)\psi ^{\dagger}(x^{'})V(x-x^{'})\psi(x)
\end{align}$$
所以：
$$[\psi ^{\dagger}(x^{'}),H]=\left( - \frac{1}{2m}\nabla^{2}_{x^{'}}+U (x^{'})\right)\psi ^{\dagger}(x^{'})+\int d^{d}x \psi ^{\dagger}(x)\psi ^{\dagger}(x^{'})V(x-x^{'})\psi(x)$$
那么：
$$\begin{align}
[\psi(x^{'}),H] & = [H,\psi ^{\dagger}(x^{'})]^{\dagger} \\
 & = - [\psi ^{\dagger}(x^{'}),H]^{\dagger} \\
 & = \left( - \frac{1}{2m}\nabla^{2}_{x^{'}}+U(x^{'}) \right) \psi(x^{'})+ \int d^{d}x \psi ^{\dagger}(x)V(x-x^{'})\psi(x^{'})\psi(x)
\end{align}$$
于是：
$$\begin{align}
\frac{\partial \rho}{\partial t} & = \frac{\partial \psi ^{\dagger}}{\partial t}\psi+ \psi ^{\dagger} \frac{\partial \psi}{\partial t} \\
 & = \frac{1}{i\hbar}[\psi ^{\dagger},H]\psi+ \psi ^{\dagger} \frac{1}{i\hbar}[\psi,H] \\
 & = - \frac{\nabla}{2mi} \cdot [\psi ^{\dagger}(x)\nabla \psi(x)-(\nabla \psi ^{\dagger}(x))\psi(x)]
\end{align}$$
于是得到粒子流密度：
$$\boxed{\mathbf{j}= \frac{1}{2mi}[\psi ^{\dagger}(x)\nabla \psi(x)-(\nabla \psi ^{\dagger}(x))\psi(x)]}$$





现在我们有$\frac{\partial \rho_{H}}{\partial t}=-\nabla \cdot j_{H}$，为了证明切换到Heisenberg绘景是合理的，我们试图证明$\frac{\partial\langle \rho\rangle}{\partial t}=- \nabla \cdot\langle \mathbf{j}\rangle$。












现在加上电场。我们将电场吸收进$\mathbf{A}$中，并且选取Coulomb规范$\nabla \cdot \mathbf{A}=0$。容易证明Coulomb规范下的对易关系：
$$\begin{align}
[\partial_{i},A_{i}] & =\partial_{i}A_{i}-A_{i}\partial_{i} \\
 & = A_{i}\partial_{i}+(\partial_{i}A_{i})-A_{i}\partial_{i}  \\
 & = (\partial_{i}A_{i})=0
\end{align}$$
那么动能项变为：
$$\begin{align}
\int d^{d}x \psi ^{\dagger}(x) \frac{(\mathbf{p}-e\mathbf{A})^{2}}{2m}\psi(x) & = \int d^{d}x \psi ^{\dagger}(x)\left( - \frac{1}{2m}\nabla^{2} + \frac{e^{2}}{2m}A^{2}-  \frac{ie\nabla \cdot \mathbf{A}}{2m} -  \frac{ie\mathbf{A}\cdot \nabla}{2m}  \right)\psi(x) \\
 & = \int d^{d}x \psi ^{\dagger}\left( - \frac{1}{2m}\nabla^{2}+ \frac{e^{2}}{2m}A^{2}- \frac{ie\mathbf{A}\cdot \nabla}{m} \right)\psi
\end{align}$$
我们假设$\mathbf{A}$和场算符$\psi ^{\dagger},\psi$是对易的。因为$\mathbf{A}$是外场的参数。定义顺磁流：
$$\boxed{\mathbf{j}^{\text{par}}_{e}= \frac{e}{2mi}[\psi ^{\dagger}(x)\nabla \psi(x)-(\nabla \psi ^{\dagger}(x))\psi(x)]}$$
我们容易发现：
$$\begin{align}
\int d^{d}x(-\mathbf{j}^{\text{par}}_{e}\cdot \mathbf{A}) & = - \frac{e}{2mi}\int d^{d}x[\psi ^{\dagger}\mathbf{A}\cdot \nabla \psi-(\mathbf{A}\cdot \nabla \psi ^{\dagger})\psi] \\
\end{align}$$
而：
$$\begin{align}
\int d^{d}x (\mathbf{A}\cdot \nabla \psi ^{\dagger})\psi & = \int d^{d}x \nabla \cdot(\mathbf{A}\psi ^{\dagger}\psi)- \int d^{d}x \psi ^{\dagger}\nabla \cdot(\mathbf{A}\psi) \\
 & = - \int d^{d}x \psi ^{\dagger}\nabla \cdot(\mathbf{A}\psi) \\
 & = -\int d^{d}x \psi ^{\dagger}\psi \nabla \cdot \mathbf{A}- \int d^{d}x \psi ^{\dagger}\mathbf{A}\cdot \nabla \psi \\
 & = -\int d^{d}x \psi ^{\dagger}\mathbf{A}\cdot \nabla \psi
\end{align}$$
所以：
$$\int d^{d}x(- \mathbf{j}^{\text{par}}_{e}\cdot \mathbf{A})= \int d^{d}x \psi ^{\dagger}\left( - \frac{ie\mathbf{A}\cdot \nabla}{m} \right)\psi$$
类似地，定义抗磁流：
$$\mathbf{j}^{dia}_{e}= - \frac{e}{2m}\mathbf{A}\psi ^{\dagger}(x)\psi(x)$$
