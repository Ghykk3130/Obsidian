考虑无自旋相互作用电子气的hamiltonian：
$$\begin{align}
H & = \int d^{d}x\psi ^{\dagger}(x)\left( - \frac{\hbar^{2}}{2m}\nabla^{2} \right)\psi(x)+ \frac{1}{2}\int d^{d}x d^{d}y \psi ^{\dagger}(x)\psi ^{\dagger}(y)V(x-y)\psi(y)\psi(x)
\end{align}$$
现在加上电场。我们将电场吸收进$\mathbf{A}$中，并且选取Coulomb规范。容易证明Coulomb规范下的对易关系：
$$\begin{align}
[\partial_{i},A_{j}] & =\partial_{i}A_{j}-A_{j}\partial_{i} \\
 & = A_{j}\partial_{i}+(\partial_{i}A_{j})-A_{j}\partial_{i} 
\end{align}$$
