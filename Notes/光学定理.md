我们先给出一些复分析的定义和定理：

>[!Note] Definition 1
>Let $D\subset \mathbb{C}$ be an open set. Let $f:D\rightarrow \mathbb{C}$ be a function. 
>- We say $f$ is holomorphic if $f$ is differentiable on $D$.
>- We say $f$ is analytic if given $z_{0}\in D$, $\exists U \ni z_{0}$ open such that $f(z)=\sum_{n=0}^{\infty}a_{n}(z-z_{0})^{n},\ \forall z\in U$,

可以证明，$f$全纯当且仅当$f$解析。

>[!Success] Theorem 1
>Let $D\subset \mathbb{C}$ be a simply connected open set. Let $f$ be a holomorphic function over $D$. Then for any closed loop $C\subset D$, we have:
>$$\oint_{C}fdz=0$$

容易看出，若$f$在单连通开集$D$内全纯，那么在$D$内两点间任意路径积分相等。接下来我们证明：


## Proof.

任取可积解析函数$f(z)$。那么：
$$\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}dx \frac{f(x)}{x+i\epsilon}= \oint_{\text{CCW}} dz \frac{f(z)}{z+i\epsilon}$$
