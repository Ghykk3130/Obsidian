我们先给出一些复分析的定义和定理：

>[!Note] Definition 1
>Let $D\subset \mathbb{C}$ be an open set. Let $f:D\rightarrow \mathbb{C}$ be a function. 
>- We say $f$ is holomorphic if $f$ is differentiable on $D$.
>- We say $f$ is analytic if given $z_{0}\in D$, $\exists U \ni z_{0}$ open such that $f(z)=\sum_{n=0}^{\infty}a_{n}(z-z_{0})^{n},\ \forall z\in U$,

可以证明，$f$全纯当且仅当$f$解析。

>[!Success] Theorem 1
>Let $D\subset \mathbb{C}$ be a simply connected open set. Let $f$ be a holomorphic function over $D$. Then for any closed loop $C\subset D$, we have:
>$$\oint_{C}fdz=0$$

容易看出，若$f$在单连通开集$D$内全纯，那么在$D$内两点间任意路径积分相等。我们再定义主值分布：

>[!Note] Definition 2
>Given $\phi(x)$, define $\text{Pr}\left(  \frac{1}{x} \right)$ by:
>$$\left\langle  \text{Pr}\left( \frac{1}{x} \right), \phi \right\rangle= \lim_{ \delta \to 0^{+} } \int_{|x|>\delta} dx \frac{\phi(x)}{x}$$

接下来我们证明：

>[!Success] Proposition 1
>$$\frac{1}{x\pm i\epsilon}\rightarrow \text{Pr}\left( \frac{1}{x} \right)\mp i\pi\delta(x)\text{, as }\epsilon\rightarrow 0^{+}$$

## Proof.

任取可积解析函数$f(z)$。那么：
$$\int_{\text{CCW}}dz \frac{f(z)}{z+i\epsilon}= \int_{-\infty}^{\infty} dx \frac{f(x)}{x+i\epsilon}+\int_{\infty}^{\delta}dx \frac{f(x)}{x+i\epsilon}+\int_{-\delta}^{-\infty} dx \frac{f(x)}{x+i\epsilon}+  \int_{\text{semicircle}} dz \frac{f(z)}{z+i\epsilon} = -2\pi i f(-i\epsilon)$$
![[Pasted image 20260422203908.png|center|500]]
若$\epsilon$很小，则$\int_{\text{semicircle}}dz \frac{f(z)}{z+i\epsilon}=-\pi i f(-i\epsilon)$。那么：
$$\begin{align}
\int_{-\infty}^{\infty} dx \frac{f(x)}{x+i\epsilon}=\int_{-\infty}^{-\delta}dx \frac{f(x)}{x+i\epsilon}+ \int_{\delta}^{\infty}dx \frac{f(x)}{x+i\epsilon}-\pi if(-i\epsilon)
\end{align}$$
取$\epsilon\rightarrow 0^{+}$得到：
$$\begin{align}
\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}dx \frac{f(x)}{x+i\epsilon}= \int_{-\infty}^{-\delta}dx \frac{f(x)}{x}+ \int_{\delta}^{\infty} dx \frac{f(x)}{x}-\pi if(0)
\end{align}$$
两边再取$\delta\rightarrow 0^{+}$，得到：
$$\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}dx \frac{f(x)}{x+i\epsilon}= \left\langle  \text{Pr}\left(  \frac{1}{x} \right), f \right\rangle- i\pi\int_{-\infty}^{\infty} dx f(x)\delta(x)$$
所以：
$$\frac{1}{x+i\epsilon}=\text{Pr}\left(  \frac{1}{x} \right)-i\pi\delta(x)$$
同理可得：
$$\frac{1}{x-i\epsilon}=\text{Pr}\left( \frac{1}{x} \right)+i\pi\delta(x)$$
>[!Right]
>$\blacksquare$

接下来证明光学定理。考虑：
$$\begin{align}
 & \ket{\psi} = \frac{1}{E-H_{0}+i\epsilon}V\ket{\psi} +\ket{\mathbf{k}}  \\
\implies &  \bra{\psi} V \ket{\psi} = \bra{\psi}  V \frac{1}{E-H_{0}-i\epsilon}V \ket{\psi} +\bra{\mathbf{k}} V\ket{\psi}   \\
\implies &  \bra{\mathbf{k}} V \ket{\psi} = \bra{\psi}V \ket{\psi} -\bra{\psi} V \frac{1}{E-H_{0}-i\epsilon}V \ket{\psi}  
\end{align}$$

>[!Quote] 如何理解$\frac{1}{E-H_{0}+i\epsilon}$？
>选取基使得$H_{0}$对角化。所以$E-H_{0}+i\epsilon$就是一个对角阵。$\frac{1}{E-H_{0}+i\epsilon}$就是这个对角阵的所有元求逆。所以自然$\left( \frac{1}{E-H_{0}+i\epsilon} \right)^{\dagger}= \frac{1}{E-H_{0}-i\epsilon}$。

由于$\bra{\psi}V \ket{\psi}\in \mathbb{R}$，所以：
$$\mathrm{Im}(\bra{\mathbf{k}} V \ket{\psi} )= -\mathrm{Im}\left( \bra{\psi} V \frac{1}{E-H_{0}-i\epsilon}V\ket{\psi}  \right)$$
注意到：
$$\begin{align}
\bra{\psi} V \frac{1}{E-H_{0}-i\epsilon}V\ket{\psi}  & = \int d^{3}k^{'} |\bra{\psi} V \ket{\mathbf{k}^{'}}  | ^{2}\frac{1}{E- \frac{\hbar^{2}k^{'2}}{2m}-i\epsilon}  \\
 & = \int d^{3}k^{'}|\bra{\psi} V \ket{\mathbf{k}^{'}}  | ^{2}\left[ \text{Pr}\left( \frac{1}{E- \frac{\hbar^{2}k^{'2}}{2m}} \right)+ \pi i \delta\left( E- \frac{\hbar^{2}k^{'2}}{2m} \right) \right]
\end{align}$$
取虚部得到：
$$\begin{align}
\mathrm{Im}(\bra{\mathbf{k}} V \ket{\psi} ) & = -\pi \int d^{3}k^{'}|\bra{\psi} V \ket{\mathbf{k}^{'}}  |^{2}  \delta\left( E- \frac{\hbar^{2}k^{'2}}{2m} \right) \\
 & = -\pi \int d\Omega^{'} dk^{'}k^{'2} \left( \frac{\hbar^{2}}{4\pi^{2}m} \right)^{2} |f(\mathbf{k}^{'},\mathbf{k})|^{2}\delta\left( E- \frac{\hbar^{2}k^{'2}}{2m} \right) \\
 & = - \frac{\hbar^{4}}{16\pi^{3}m^{2}} \int dk^{'}k^{'2} d\sigma \delta\left( E- \frac{\hbar^{2}k^{'2}}{2m} \right) \\
 & = - \frac{\hbar^{4}}{16\pi^{3}m^{2}} \sigma_{\text{TOT}}\cdot 2\int \frac{1}{2}k^{'}dk^{'2}\delta\left( \frac{2mE}{\hbar^{2}}-k^{'2} \right) \frac{2m}{\hbar^{2}} \\
 & = - \frac{\hbar^{2}}{8\pi^{3}m}\sigma_{\text{TOT}}k
\end{align}$$
而$f(\mathbf{k},\mathbf{k})= - \frac{4\pi^{2}m}{\hbar^{2}} \bra{\mathbf{k}}V \ket{\psi}$。所以：
$$\mathrm{Im}(f(\mathbf{k},\mathbf{k}))= \frac{k}{2\pi }\sigma_{\text{TOT}}$$
