# 1. 生成湮灭算符

在占据数表象下，我们定义Fock空间：
$$F=F_{1} \oplus F_{2} \oplus\dots$$
其中$F_{j}=\left\{  \ket{n_{1},n_{2},\dots}|\sum_{i}n_{i}=j  \right\}$。

假设对于玻色子存在$a\ket{n}=\sqrt{ n }\ket{n-1},\ a^{\dagger}\ket{n}=\sqrt{ n+1 }\ket{n+1}$，对于费米子存在$a\ket{n}=\sqrt{ n }\ket{n-1},\ a^{\dagger}\ket{n}=\sqrt{ 1-n }\ket{n+1}$。我们可以唯一定义占据数表象下的态：

>[!Note] Definition 1.1
>$$\ket{n_{1},n_{2},\dots} = \frac{(a_{1}^{\dagger})^{n_{1}}}{\sqrt{ n_{1}! }} \frac{(a_{2}^{\dagger})^{n_{2}}}{\sqrt{ n_{2}! }}\dots \ket{0} $$

那么假设如下对易关系存在：
$$[a_{i},a_{j}^{\dagger}]_{\pm}=\delta_{ij},\ [a_{i},a_{j}]_{\pm}=0,\ [a_{i}^{\dagger},a_{j}^{\dagger}]=0$$
其中$\pm$表示正反对易关系，玻色子取正，费米子取负。反对易关系指$\{  \}$，正对易关系指$[]$。那么对于玻色子：
$$\begin{align}
  a_{j}\ket{n_{1},\dots,n_{j},\dots} & =  a_{j} \frac{(a_{1}^{\dagger})^{n_{1}}}{\sqrt{ n_{1}! }} \dots\frac{(a_{j}^{\dagger})^{n_{j}}}{\sqrt{ n_{j}! }}\dots \ket{0} \\
 & = \frac{(a_{1}^{\dagger})^{n_{1}} }{\sqrt{ n_{1}! } }\dots a_{j} \frac{(a_{j}^{\dagger})^{n_{j}}}{\sqrt{ n_{j}! } }\dots\ket{0} \\
 & = \frac{(a_{1}^{\dagger})^{n_{1}}}{\sqrt{ n_{1}! }}\dots \sqrt{ n_{j}  }\ket{0,\dots,n_{j}-1,\dots}  \\
  & = \sqrt{ n_{j} }\ket{n_{1},\dots,n_{j}-1,\dots} 
\end{align}$$
同理：
$$a_{j}^{\dagger}\ket{n_{1},\dots,n_{\mathbf{j}},\dots}=\sqrt{ n_{j}+1 } \ket{n_{1},\dots,n_{j}+1,\dots} $$
对于费米子：
$$\begin{align}
a_{j}\ket{n_{1},\dots,n_{j},\dots}  & = a_{j}\frac{(a_{1}^{\dagger})^{n_{1}}}{\sqrt{ n_{1}! }}\dots \frac{(a_{j}^{\dagger})^{n_{j}}}{\sqrt{ n_{j}! }}\dots \ket{0} \\
 & = (-1)^{\nu} \frac{(a_{1}^{\dagger})^{n_{1}}}{\sqrt{ n_{1}! }}\dots a_{j} \frac{(a_{j}^{\dagger})^{n_{j}} }{\sqrt{ n_{j}! }}\dots \ket{0} \\
 & = (-1)^{\nu}\sqrt{ n_{j} }\ket{0,\dots,n_{j}-1,\dots}\text{, where }\nu=\sum_{i=1 }^{j-1}n_{i}    
\end{align}$$
这是因为每交换一次，就会引出一个负号。类似地：
$$a_{j}^{\dagger}\ket{n_{1},\dots,n_{j},\dots}= (-1)^{\nu}\sqrt{ 1-n_{j} } \ket{n_{1},\dots,n_{j},\dots} $$
我们提出如下作用规则：

>[!Note] Postulate 1.1
>We assume that:
>$$[a_{i},a_{j}^{\dagger}]_{\pm}=\delta_{ij},\ [a_{i},a_{j}]_{\pm}=0,\ [a_{i}^{\dagger},a_{j}^{\dagger}]_{\pm}=0$$
>For bosons:
>$$a_{j}\ket{n_{1},\dots,n_{j},\dots} =\sqrt{ n_{j} }\ket{n_{1},\dots,n_{j}-1,\dots} ,\ a_{j}^{\dagger}\ket{n_{1},\dots,n_{j},\dots} = \sqrt{ n_{j_{}}+1 }\ket{n_{1},\dots,n_{j}+1,\dots} $$
>For fermions:
>$$a_{j}\ket{n_{1},\dots,n_{j},\dots} =(-1)^{\nu}\sqrt{ n_{j} }\ket{n_{1},\dots,n_{j}-1,\dots} ,\ a_{j}^{\dagger}\ket{n_{1},\dots,n_{j},\dots} = (-1)^{\nu}\sqrt{ n_{j_{}}+1 }\ket{n_{1},\dots,n_{j}+1,\dots} $$
## Ex:

令$H=\omega a^{\dagger}a+\epsilon c^{\dagger}c+ga^{\dagger}ac^{\dagger}c$，其中$a,c$为费米子算符。则态空间为$\ket{n_{a},n_{c}}$。容易看出，$H$是对角化的：
$$H\ket{n_{a},n_{c}} = (\omega n_{a}+\epsilon n_{c}+gn_{a}n_{c})\ket{n_{a},n_{c}} $$

将生成算符作用在真空态上得到一次量子化下的态：
$$\ket{j} =a^{\dagger}_{j}\ket{0} $$
那么可将生成算符写为：
$$a^{\dagger}_{j}=\ket{j} \bra{0} $$
相应地，湮灭算符可以写为$a_{j}=\ket{0}\bra{j}$。假设存在两组不同的基$\{ \ket{j} \},\{ \ket{l} \}$，对应两组不同的生成湮灭算符，我们有：
$$\begin{align}
a_{j} & = \ket{0} \bra{j}  \\
 & = \sum_{l}\ket{0} \bra{j} l\rangle\bra{l}  \\
 & = \sum_{l}\bra{j} l\rangle a_{l}
\end{align}$$
类似地：
$$\begin{align}
a^{\dagger}_{j} & = \ket{j} \bra{0}  \\
 & = \sum_{l}\ket{l} \bra{l} j\rangle\bra{0} \\
 & = \sum_{l}\bra{l} j\rangle a^{\dagger}_{l} 
\end{align}$$
# 2. 场算符

考虑单粒子态$\ket{x}$。称生成$\ket{x}$的算符为场算符。即：
$$\psi ^{\dagger}(x)\ket{0} =\ket{x} $$
容易将场算符表达为生成湮灭算符：
$$\begin{align}
 & \psi ^{\dagger}(x)  = \sum_{j}\bra{j} x\rangle a_{j}^{\dagger} \\
 & \psi(x)= \sum_{j}\bra{x}j\rangle a_{j} 
\end{align}$$
那么可以得到场算符对易关系：
$$\begin{align}
[\psi ^{}(x),\psi ^{\dagger}(y) ]_{\pm}  & = \left[ \sum_{i}\bra{x} i\rangle  a_{i}^{},\sum_{j}\bra{y} j\rangle a_{j}^{\dagger}\right]_{\pm} \\
 & = \sum_{i,j}\bra{x} i\rangle \bra{y}j\rangle [a_{i},a_{j}^{\dagger}]_{\pm} \\
 & = \sum_{i,j}\bra{x} i\rangle \bra{y} j\rangle \delta_{ij} \\
 & = \delta(x-y) 
\end{align}$$
$$\begin{align}
 & [\psi ^{\dagger}(x),\psi ^{\dagger}(y)]_{\pm}  = \sum_{i,j}\bra{i} x\rangle \bra{j} y\rangle [a_{i}^{\dagger},a_{j}^{\dagger}]_{\pm}=0 \\
 &  [\psi(x),\psi(y)]_{\pm}= \sum_{i,j} \bra{x} i\rangle \bra{y} j\rangle [a_{i},a_{j}]_{\pm}=0
\end{align}$$
>[!Success] Proposition 2.1
>The commutation relations for field operators are:
>$$\begin{align}
 & [\psi(x),\psi ^{\dagger}(y)]_{\pm} = \delta(x-y) \\
 & [\psi ^{\dagger}(x,\psi ^{\dagger}(y))]_{\pm}= 0,\ [\psi(x),\psi(y)]_{\pm}=0
\end{align}$$
# 3.单体算符二次量子化

令$\mathcal{O}=\sum_{j}\mathcal{o}^{j}$为单体算符。例如总动能就是单体算符。不妨令其本征态为$\ket{\lambda}$。我们希望系统处于$\ket{n_{\lambda_{1}},\dots}$时，该可观测量为$\sum_{\lambda}o_{\lambda_{},\lambda_{}}n_{\lambda_{}}$。此处因为对于不动粒子$j$来说，矩阵元$o_{\lambda_{},\lambda_{}}$都一样，所以省略$j$。那么可以令$\mathcal{O}$为：
$$\mathcal{O}=\sum_{\lambda}o_{\lambda_{},\lambda_{}} n_{\lambda_{}}=\sum_{\lambda}o_{\lambda_{},\lambda_{}}a^{\dagger}_{\lambda_{}}a_{\lambda_{}}$$
若$o$在基$\{ \ket{\alpha} \}$下不对角，我们也可以表示$\mathcal{O}$：
$$\begin{align}
\mathcal{O} & = \sum_{\lambda}o_{\lambda,\lambda}a_{\lambda}^{\dagger}a_{\lambda} \\
 & = \sum_{\lambda}o_{\lambda,\lambda}\sum_{\alpha,\beta} \bra{\alpha} \lambda\rangle \bra{\lambda} \beta\rangle a_{\alpha}^{\dagger} a_{\beta} \\
 & = \sum_{\alpha,\beta}o_{\alpha,\beta}a_{\alpha}^{\dagger}a_{\beta}
\end{align}$$

>[!Success] Proposition 3.1
>Let $\mathcal{O}=\sum_{i}o^{i}$ be s one-body operator. Then:
>$$\mathcal{O}=\sum_{\alpha,\beta}o_{\alpha,\beta}a^{\dagger}_{\alpha}a_{\beta}$$
## Ex:

考虑单体算符$H= \sum_{j} (\frac{p_{j}^{2}}{2m}+V(x_{j}))$。我们计算：
$$\bra{x} \left( \frac{p^{2}}{2m}+V(x) \right)\ket{y} = \left( - \frac{\nabla^{2}}{2m}+V(x) \right)\delta(x-y)$$
那么：
$$\begin{align}
H & = \int d^{d}x d^{d}y \left[ \left( - \frac{\nabla^{2}}{2m}+V(x) \right)\delta(x-y) \right]\psi ^{\dagger}(x)\psi(y) \\
 & = \int d^{d}x\left[ \left( - \frac{\nabla^{2}}{2m}+V(x) \right)\left( \int d^{d}y \delta(x-y)\psi(y) \right) \right]\psi ^{\dagger}(x) \\
 & = \int d^{d}x \psi ^{\dagger}(x)\left( - \frac{\nabla^{2}}{2m}+V(x) \right)\psi(x)
\end{align}$$
## Ex:

考虑粒子密度算符$\rho(x)=\sum_{j}\delta(x-x_{j})$。可将$x$视为一个参数，一个粒子的密度算符为$\delta(x-x^{'})$。那么：
$$\bra{x^{'}} \delta(x-x^{'})\ket{x^{''}} = \delta(x-x^{'})\delta(x^{'}-x^{''})$$
我们有：
$$\begin{align}
\rho(x) & = \int d^{d}x^{'}d^{d}x^{''} \delta(x-x^{'})\delta(x^{'}-x^{''}) \psi ^{\dagger}(x^{'})\psi(x^{''}) \\
 & = \psi ^{\dagger}(x)\psi(x)
\end{align}$$
可以得到总粒子数算符：
$$N=\int d^{d}x \rho(x)=\int d^{d}x\psi ^{\dagger}(x)\psi(x)$$
# 2. 两体算符的二次量子化

考虑一个两体算符$V=\sum_{i< j}v(x_{i},x_{j})= \frac{1}{2}\sum_{i\neq j}v(x_{i},x_{j})$。我们希望$V$作用在$\ket{1_{x_{1}},\dots,1_{x_{N}}}=\ket{x_{1},\dots,x_{N}}$上得到$\frac{1}{2}\sum_{i\neq j}v(x_{i},x_{j})\ket{x_{1},\dots,x_{N}}$。为此，我们猜测：
$$V= \frac{1}{2}\int d^{d}x d^{d}y\psi ^{\dagger}(x)\psi ^{\dagger}(y)v(x,y)\psi(y)\psi(x)$$
我们对费米子来验证这一点。注意到：
$$\begin{align}
\psi(x)\ket{x_{1},\dots,x_{N}}  & = \psi(x)\psi ^{\dagger}(x_{1})\dots \psi ^{\dagger}(x_{N})\ket{0}  \\
 & = \delta(x-x_{1})\psi ^{\dagger}(x_{2})\dots \psi ^{\dagger}(x_{N})\ket{0} -\psi ^{\dagger}(x_{1})\psi(x)\dots \psi ^{\dagger}(x_{N})\ket{0} 
\end{align}$$
我们通过对易关系一直把$\psi(x)$往右移，最终和$\ket{0}$作用生成零矢量。即：
$$\begin{align}
\psi(x)\ket{x_{1},\dots,x_{N}}  & = \delta(x-x_{1})\psi ^{\dagger}(x_{2})\dots \psi ^{\dagger}(x_{N})\ket{0} -\psi ^{\dagger}(x_{1})\psi(x)\dots \psi ^{\dagger}(x_{N})\ket{0} \\
 & = \delta(x-x_{1})\psi ^{\dagger}(x_{2})\dots \psi ^{\dagger}(x_{N})\ket{0}- \delta(x-x_{2}) \psi ^{\dagger}(x_{1})\psi ^{\dagger}(x_{3})\dots \psi ^{\dagger}(x_{N})\ket{0} + \psi ^{\dagger}(x_{1})\psi ^{\dagger}(x_{2})\psi(x)\dots \psi ^{\dagger}(x_{N})\ket{0}  \\
 & =\dots \\
 & = \delta(x-x_{1})\psi ^{\dagger}(x_{2})\dots \psi ^{\dagger}(x_{N})\ket{0} -\delta(x-x_{2})\psi ^{\dagger}(x_{1})\psi ^{\dagger}(x_{3})\dots \psi ^{\dagger}(x_{N} )\ket{0} +\dots+(-1)^{N-1}\delta(x-x_{N})\psi ^{\dagger}(x_{1})\dots \psi ^{\dagger}(x_{N-1})\ket{0}  \\
 & = \sum_{j}(-1)^{j-1}\delta(x-x_{j})\psi ^{\dagger}(x_{1})\dots  \widehat{\psi ^{\dagger}(x_{j})}\dots \psi ^{\dagger}(x_{N})\ket{0} 
\end{align}$$
其中$\hat{\phantom{w}}$表示hat下面的元素不存在。接下来：
$$\begin{align}
\psi ^{\dagger}(x)\psi ^{\dagger}(y)v(x,y)\psi(y) \sum_{j}(-1)^{j-1} \delta(x-x_{j}) \psi ^{\dagger}(x_{1})\dots \widehat{\psi ^{\dagger}(x_{j})}\dots \psi ^{\dagger}(x_{N})\ket{0} & = \psi ^{\dagger}(y)v(x,y)\psi(y)\psi ^{\dagger}(x) \sum_{j}(-1)^{j-1} \delta(x-x_{j}) \psi ^{\dagger}(x_{1})\dots \widehat{\psi ^{\dagger}(x_{j})}\dots \psi ^{\dagger}(x_{N})\ket{0} \\
\end{align}$$
这是因为，我们规定$v(x,x)=0$以保证不对同一个粒子自身求和。那么可以假设$x\neq y$，于是对易关系只会产生一个负号。将$\psi ^{\dagger}(x)$移过$\psi ^{\dagger}(y),\psi(y)$分别产生一个负号，抵消了。接下来：
$$\begin{align}
\psi ^{\dagger}(x)\sum_{j}(-1)^{j-1}\delta(x-x_{j})  \psi ^{\dagger}(x_{1})\dots  \widehat{\psi ^{\dagger}(x_{j})}\dots \psi ^{\dagger}(x_{N})\ket{0}   & =\psi ^{\dagger}(x_{j})\sum_{j}(-1)^{j-1}\delta(x-x_{j})  \psi ^{\dagger}(x_{1})\dots  \widehat{\psi ^{\dagger}(x_{j})}\dots \psi ^{\dagger}(x_{N})\ket{0}     \\
 & = \sum_{j}\delta(x-x_{j}) \ket{x_{1},\dots,x_{N}} 
\end{align}$$
这是因为我们将$\psi ^{\dagger}(x_{j})$移动到原来位置，又会多出$(-1)^{j-1}$，使得负号抵消。那么：
$$\begin{align}
V\ket{x_{1},\dots,x_{N}}  & = \frac{1}{2}\int d^{d}y v(x,y)\psi ^{\dagger}(y)\psi(y) \sum_{j}\delta(x-x_{j})\ket{x_{1},\dots,x_{N}}  \\
 & = \frac{1}{2}\sum_{j} \int d^{d}yv(x_{j},y)\psi ^{\dagger}(y)\psi(y)\ket{x_{1},\dots,x_{N}} 
\end{align}$$
对于$y$作同样的操作，显然可以得到：
$$V= \frac{1}{2}\sum_{i,j}v(x_{j},x_{i})\ket{x_{1},\dots,x_{N}}= \frac{1}{2}\sum_{i\neq j}v(x_{j},x_{i})\ket{x_{1},\dots,x_{N}}  $$
对于玻色子，没有负号，上述验证过程更加容易。一般来说，若$\ket{k},\ket{q}$为本征态：
$$V= \frac{1}{2} \sum_{k,q}\bra{k,q} v\ket{k,q} a^{\dagger}_{k}a^{\dagger}_{q}a_{q}a_{k}$$
在另外的基下：
$$\begin{align}
V & = \frac{1}{2} \sum_{k,q}\sum_{\alpha,\beta,\delta,\gamma}\bra{k,q} v\ket{k,q} \bra{\alpha} k\rangle \bra{\beta} q\rangle \bra{q} \delta\rangle \bra{k} \gamma\rangle a^{\dagger}_{\alpha}a^{\dagger}_{\beta} a_{\delta}a_{\gamma} \\
 &= \frac{1}{2} \sum_{\alpha,\beta,\delta,\gamma}\sum_{k,q}\bra{\alpha,\beta} k,q\rangle \bra{k,q} v\ket{k,q} \bra{k,q} \gamma,\delta\rangle a^{\dagger}_{\alpha}a^{\dagger}_{\beta}a_{\delta}a_{\gamma} \\
 & = \frac{1}{2} \sum_{\alpha,\beta,\delta,\gamma} \bra{\alpha,\beta} v\ket{\gamma,\delta} a^{\dagger}_{\alpha}a^{\dagger}_{\beta}a_{\delta}a_{\gamma}
\end{align}$$

>[!Success] Proposition 2.1
>Let $V=\sum_{i\neq j}v(x_{i},x_{j})$ be a two-body operator. Then:
>$$V= \frac{1}{2} \sum_{\alpha,\beta,\delta,\gamma} \bra{\alpha,\beta} v\ket{\gamma,\delta} a^{\dagger}_{\alpha}a^{\dagger}_{\beta}a_{\delta}a_{\gamma}$$

## Ex:

考虑库伦作用$V= \frac{1}{2}\sum_{i\neq j}v(\mathbf{r}_{i},\mathbf{r}_{j})$, $v(\mathbf{r}_{i},\mathbf{r}_{j})= \frac{1}{4\pi\epsilon_{0}} \frac{e^{2}}{|\mathbf{r}_{i}-\mathbf{r}_{j}|}$。我们计算：
$$\begin{align}
\bra{\mathbf{k}_{1},\mathbf{k}_{2} } v \ket{\mathbf{k}_{3},\mathbf{k}_{4}}  & = \frac{1}{4\pi\epsilon_{0}}  \int d^{3}r_{i}d^{3}r_{j} \bra{\mathbf{k}_{1},\mathbf{k}_{2}}  \mathbf{r}_{i},\mathbf{r}_{j}\rangle \bra{\mathbf{r}_{i},\mathbf{r}_{j}}  \frac{e^{2}}{|\mathbf{r}_{j}-\mathbf{r}_{j}|}\ket{\mathbf{k}_{3},\mathbf{k}_{4}}  \\
 & = \frac{1}{4\pi\epsilon_{0}} \frac{1}{V^{2}} \int d^{3}r_{i}d^{3}r_{j} e^{-i\mathbf{r}_{i}\cdot \mathbf{k}_{1}} e^{-i\mathbf{r}_{j}\cdot \mathbf{k}_{2}} e^{i\mathbf{k}_{3}\cdot \mathbf{r}_{i}}e^{i\mathbf{k}_{4}\cdot \mathbf{r}_{j}} \frac{e^{2}}{|\mathbf{r}_{i}-\mathbf{r}_{j}|} 
\end{align}$$
我们令$\mathbf{R}_{ij}=\mathbf{r}_{i}-\mathbf{r}_{j},\ \mathbf{r}_{ij}=\mathbf{r}_{i}-\mathbf{r}_{j}$。容易得到Jacobian：
$$\frac{\partial(\mathbf{R}_{ij},\mathbf{r}_{ij})}{\partial(\mathbf{r}_{i},\mathbf{r}_{j})}=\begin{pmatrix}
\text{diag}(1,1,1) & \text{diag}(-1,-1,-1) \\
\text{diag(1,1,1)} & \text{diag(1,1,1)}
\end{pmatrix}$$
行列式为$8$。故：
$$\begin{align}
\bra{\mathbf{k}_{1},\mathbf{k}_{2}} v \ket{\mathbf{k}_{3},\mathbf{k}_{4}}  & =   \frac{2}{\pi\epsilon_{0}}  \frac{1}{V^{2}} \int d^{3}r_{ij}d^{3}R_{ij} \exp\left( i \frac{\mathbf{R}_{ij}}{2}\cdot(-\mathbf{k}_{1}+\mathbf{k}_{2}+\mathbf{k}_{3}-\mathbf{k}_{4}) \right)\exp\left( i \frac{\mathbf{r}_{ij}}{2}\cdot(-\mathbf{k}_{1}-\mathbf{k}_{2}+\mathbf{k}_{3}+\mathbf{k}_{4} )\right) \frac{e^{2}}{R_{ij}} \\
 & = \frac{2}{\pi\epsilon_{0}V^{2}}\int d^{3}R_{ij}\exp\left( i \frac{\mathbf{R}_{ij}}{2}\cdot(-\mathbf{k}_{1}+\mathbf{k}_{2}+\mathbf{k}_{3}-\mathbf{k}_{4}) \right)V \delta(-\mathbf{k}_{1}-\mathbf{k}_{2}+\mathbf{k}_{3}+\mathbf{k}_{4}=0) \frac{e^{2}}{R_{ij}} \\

\end{align}$$
令$\mathbf{q}=\mathbf{k}_{1}-\mathbf{k}_{3}=\mathbf{k}_{4}-\mathbf{k}_{2}$。令$\mathbf{k}_{3}=\mathbf{k},\ \mathbf{k}_{4}=\mathbf{p}$。则有：
$$\begin{align}
\bra{\mathbf{k}+\mathbf{q},\mathbf{p}-\mathbf{q}} v \ket{\mathbf{k},\mathbf{p}}  & = \frac{2}{\pi\epsilon_{0}V^{}}\int d^{3}R_{ij} \exp(i \mathbf{R}_{ij}\cdot \mathbf{q} )  \frac{e^{2}}{R_{ij}} \\
 & = \frac{8e^{2}}{q^{2}V}
\end{align}$$
于是：
$$V=\sum_{\mathbf{k},\mathbf{p},\mathbf{q}} \frac{8e^{2}}{q^{2}V} a^{\dagger}_{\mathbf{k}+\mathbf{q}}a^{\dagger}_{\mathbf{p}-\mathbf{q}}a_{\mathbf{p}}a_{\mathbf{k}}$$
