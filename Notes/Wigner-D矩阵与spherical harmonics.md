
回忆起spherical harmonics为：
$$Y_{l}^m(\theta,\phi)=\bra{\theta,\phi} l,m\rangle$$
 我们有：
 $$\begin{align}
 & \ket{\hat{n}} =\ket{\theta,\phi} =\mathscr{D}^{(l)}(\alpha=\phi,\beta=\theta,0)\ket{\hat{z}} \\
 \implies & \bra{l,m} \hat{n}\rangle= \sum_{m^{'}}\bra{l,m} \mathscr{D}\ket{l,m^{'}} \bra{l,m^{'}} \hat{z}\rangle \\
\implies & Y_{l}^{m*}(\theta,\phi)= \sum_{m^{'}}\mathscr{D}^{(l)}_{m,m^{'}}Y_{l}^{m^{'}*}(\theta=0,\phi)
\end{align} $$
