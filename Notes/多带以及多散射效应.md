# 1. 多带效应

考虑多条能带具有费米面。那么总输运系数就是这些带的共同贡献。用$l$标记能带。那么：
$$\begin{align}
j^{e} & = \sum_{l}j^{e}_{l} \\
 & = \sum_{l}(\sigma_{l}E-\alpha_{l}\nabla T)=\sigma E-\alpha \nabla T \\
\implies & \sigma=\sum_{l}\sigma_{l},\ \alpha=\sum_{l}\alpha_{l}
\end{align}$$
由于$S= \frac{E}{\nabla T},\ \sigma= \frac{j^{e}}{E},\ \alpha=- \frac{j^{e}}{\nabla T}$，所以$S=- \frac{\alpha}{\sigma}$。所以：
$$\begin{align}
S & = - \frac{\sum_{l}\alpha_{l}}{\sum_{l}\sigma_{l} }  \\
 & = \frac{\sum_{l}S_{l}\sigma_{l}}{\sum_{l}\sigma_{l}}
\end{align}$$

>[!Success] Proposition 1.1
>Let $l$ be the index of the lth band. Then:
>$$\begin{align}
 & \sigma=\sum_{l}\sigma_{l} \\
 & \alpha=\sum_{l}\alpha_{l} \\
 & S= \frac{\sum_{l}S_{l}\sigma_{l}}{\sum_{l}\sigma_{l}}
\end{align}$$

# 2. 多散射效应

考虑一条能带。若该条能带存在多种散射机制，用$i$标记散射机制。我们知道若只存在一种散射机制，则在$\epsilon_{F}$附近：
$$\begin{align}
\sigma & = e^{2}K_{0} \\
 & = e^{2} \int_{-\infty}^{\infty}d\epsilon N(\epsilon)\left( - \frac{\partial f^{0}}{\partial\epsilon} \right) \frac{1}{3}v^{2}\tau \\
 & \approx e^{2}N(\epsilon) \frac{1}{3}v^{2}\tau(\epsilon)=C\tau(\epsilon)
\end{align}$$
对于第$i$种散射机制，若这种机制单独存在，我们有：
$$\begin{align}
\sigma_{i} & =C\tau_{i}
\end{align}$$
显然因为是一条能带，$C$对于任何$i$都一样。显然，总散射律是各个散射机制散射率之和：
$$\begin{align}
w & = \sum_{i}w_{i} \implies \frac{1}{\tau}=\sum_{i} \frac{1}{\tau_{i}}
\end{align}$$
那么：
$$\sigma= C\tau= \frac{C}{\sum_{i}1 /\tau_{i} }= \frac{1}{\sum_{i}1 /\sigma_{i}}$$
故：
$$\begin{align}
S & = - \frac{\pi^{2}k^{2}T}{3|e|} \left.\frac{\partial}{\partial\epsilon}(\ln \sigma)\right|_{\epsilon=\epsilon_{F}} \\
 & = - \frac{\pi^{2}k^{2}T}{3|e|} \left( - \frac{\sum_{i} \frac{\partial}{\partial\epsilon} \frac{1}{\sigma_{i}}}{\sum_{i} \frac{1}{\sigma_{i}}} \right) \\
 & = - \frac{\pi^{2}k^{2}T}{3|e|} \frac{\sum_{i} \rho_{i} \frac{1}{\sigma_{i}} \frac{\partial \sigma_{i}}{\partial\epsilon} }{\sum_{i}\rho_{i}} \\
 & = \frac{\sum_{i}\rho_{i}S_{i}}{\sum_{i}\rho_{i}}
\end{align}$$

>[!Success] Proposition 2.1 (Nordheim-Gorter rule)
>Given a single band. Let $i$ be the index of the ith scattering mechanism of the band. Then the Seebeck coefficient of the band is:
>$$S= \frac{\sum_{i}\rho_{i}S_{i}}{\sum_{i}\rho_{i}}$$
