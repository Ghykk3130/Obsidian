# 1. 二次量子化hamiltonian

考虑相互作用电子在均匀正电背景中。则电子动能与电子相互作用能为：
$$H_{\text{el}}= \sum_{i} \frac{p^{2}_{i}}{2m}+ \frac{1}{2}\sum_{i\neq j} e^{2} \frac{e^{-\mu|\mathbf{r}_{i}-\mathbf{r}_{j}|}}{|\mathbf{r}_{i}-\mathbf{r}_{j}|}$$
此处我们引入汤川势来避免电子距离为零时的奇点。在最终计算出结果，取$\mu=0$即可。电子与正电背景相互作用能为：
$$\begin{align}
H_{\text{el-b}} & =- \sum_{i} \int d^{3}x n(x) e^{2} \frac{e^{-\mu|\mathbf{r}_{i}-\mathbf{x}|}}{|\mathbf{r}_{i}-\mathbf{x}|}
\end{align}$$
正电背景的自作用能为：
$$\begin{align}
H_{\text{b}}= e^{2} \int d^{3}x d^{3}x^{'} n(\mathbf{x})n(\mathbf{x}^{'}) \frac{e^{-\mu|\mathbf{x}-\mathbf{x}^{'}|}}{|\mathbf{x}-\mathbf{x}^{'}|}
\end{align}$$
选取动量算符的本征态$\{ \ket{\mathbf{k}} \}$作为单态基底。显然：
$$\sum_{i} \frac{p^{2}_{i}}{2m}\leadsto \sum_{\mathbf{k},\sigma} \frac{k^{2}}{2m} c^{\dagger}_{\mathbf{k},\sigma}c_{\mathbf{k},\sigma}$$

>[!Quote] 只与二体距离有关的二体势的二次量子化
>考虑二体势能$V=\frac{1}{2}\sum_{i\neq j} v(|\mathbf{x}_{i}-\mathbf{x}_{j}|)$。即二体势只与二体距离有关。那么：
>$$\begin{align}
V & =   \frac{1}{2}\sum_{\mathbf{k},\sigma,\mathbf{k}^{'},\sigma^{'},\mathbf{k}^{''},\sigma^{''},\mathbf{k}^{'''},\sigma^{'''}} \bra{\mathbf{k} \sigma, \mathbf{k}^{'}\sigma^{'}} v \ket{\mathbf{k}^{''}\sigma^{''},\mathbf{k}^{'''}\sigma^{'''}} c^{\dagger}_{\mathbf{k} \sigma }c^{\dagger}_{\mathbf{k}^{'}\sigma^{'}}c_{\mathbf{k}^{'''}\sigma^{'''}}c_{\mathbf{k}^{''}\sigma^{''}}
\end{align}$$
>显然$v$不作用在自旋态上。自旋必须匹配。我们得到：
>$$\begin{align}
V= \frac{1}{2} \sum_{\mathbf{k}^{},\mathbf{k}^{'},\mathbf{k}^{''},\mathbf{k}^{'''},\sigma,\sigma^{'}}\bra{\mathbf{k},\mathbf{k}^{'}} v \ket{\mathbf{k}^{''},\mathbf{k}^{'''}} c^{\dagger}_{\mathbf{k}\sigma}c^{\dagger}_{\mathbf{k}^{'}\sigma^{'}}c_{\mathbf{k}^{'''}\sigma^{'}}c_{\mathbf{k}^{''}\sigma}
\end{align}$$
>我们还有办法得到$\mathbf{k}$的selection rule。令$\mathbf{R}=\frac{\mathbf{x}_{i}-\mathbf{x}_{j}}{2},\ \mathbf{r}= \frac{\mathbf{x}_{i}+\mathbf{x}_{j}}{2}$。我们有：
>$$\begin{align}
\bra{\mathbf{k},\mathbf{k}^{'}} v \ket{\mathbf{k}^{''},\mathbf{k}^{'''}}  & = \frac{1}{V^{2}} \int d^{3}x_{i}d^{3}x_{j} e^{-i\mathbf{k}\cdot \mathbf{x}_{i}}e^{-i\mathbf{k}^{'}\cdot \mathbf{x}_{j}} e^{i\mathbf{k}^{''}\cdot \mathbf{x}_{i}}e^{i\mathbf{k}^{'''}\cdot \mathbf{x}_{j}} v(|\mathbf{x}_{i}-\mathbf{x}_{j}|) \\
 & = \frac{8}{V^{2}} \int d^{3}r \exp(i(\mathbf{k}^{''}-\mathbf{k}+\mathbf{k}^{'''}-\mathbf{k}^{'})\cdot \mathbf{r}) \int d^{3}R  \exp(i(\mathbf{k}^{''}-\mathbf{k}-\mathbf{k}^{'''}+\mathbf{k}^{'})\cdot \mathbf{R})v(2R) \\
 & = \frac{8}{V} \delta(\mathbf{k}^{''}-\mathbf{k}+\mathbf{k}^{'''}-\mathbf{k}^{'}=0)\int d^{3}R  \exp(i(\mathbf{k}^{''}-\mathbf{k}-\mathbf{k}^{'''}+\mathbf{k}^{'})\cdot \mathbf{R})v(2R)
\end{align}$$
>令$\mathbf{k}-\mathbf{k}^{''}=\mathbf{q}=\mathbf{k}^{'''}-\mathbf{k}^{'}$。于是重新标记波矢可以得到：
>$$v_{\mathbf{q}}=\bra{\mathbf{k}^{}+\mathbf{q},\mathbf{k}^{'}-\mathbf{q}}v \ket{\mathbf{k}^{},\mathbf{k}^{'}} = \frac{8}{V} \int d^{3}R e^{-2i\mathbf{q}\cdot \mathbf{R}}v(2R)= \frac{1}{V}\int d^{3}R e^{-i\mathbf{q}\cdot \mathbf{R}}v(R)$$
>$$V= \frac{1}{2} \sum_{\mathbf{k},\mathbf{k}^{'},\mathbf{q},\sigma,\sigma^{'}}v_{\mathbf{q}} c^{\dagger}_{\mathbf{k}+\mathbf{q} \sigma} c^{\dagger}_{\mathbf{k}^{'}-\mathbf{q} \sigma^{'}}  c_{\mathbf{k}^{'} \sigma^{'}}c_{\mathbf{k} \sigma}$$

那么，容易计算：
$$\begin{align}
v_{\mathbf{q}} & = \frac{1}{V}\int d^{3}R e^{-i\mathbf{q}\cdot \mathbf{R}} e^{2} \frac{e^{-\mu R}}{R} \\
 & = \frac{1}{V} \frac{4\pi e^{2}}{\mu^{2}+q^{2}}
\end{align}$$
电子相互作用二次量子化后为：
$$\frac{1}{2}\sum_{i\neq j} e^{2} \frac{e^{-\mu|\mathbf{r}_{i}-\mathbf{r}_{j}|}}{|\mathbf{r}_{i}-\mathbf{r}_{j}|}\leadsto \frac{1}{2V}\sum_{\mathbf{k},\mathbf{k}^{'},\mathbf{q},\sigma,\sigma^{'}} \frac{4\pi e^{2}}{\mu^{2}+q^{2}} c^{\dagger}_{\mathbf{k}+\mathbf{q}\sigma}c^{\dagger}_{\mathbf{k}^{'}-\mathbf{q}\sigma^{'}}c_{\mathbf{k}^{'}\sigma^{'}}c_{\mathbf{k}\sigma} $$
我们考虑$\mathbf{q}=0$的项：
$$\begin{align}
V_{0} & =\frac{1}{2V}\sum_{\mathbf{k},\mathbf{k}^{'},\sigma,\sigma^{'}} \frac{4\pi e^{2}}{\mu^{2}} c^{\dagger}_{\mathbf{k}\sigma}c^{\dagger}_{\mathbf{k}^{'}\sigma^{'}}c_{\mathbf{k}^{'}\sigma^{'}}c_{\mathbf{k}\sigma} \\
 & = \frac{1}{2V} \frac{4\pi e^{2}}{\mu^{2}} \sum_{\mathbf{k},\mathbf{k}^{'},\sigma,\sigma^{'}} c^{\dagger}_{\mathbf{k}\sigma}c^{\dagger}_{\mathbf{k}^{'}\sigma^{'}}c_{\mathbf{k}\sigma}c_{\mathbf{k}^{'}\sigma^{'}} \\
 & = \frac{1}{2V} \frac{4\pi e^{2}}{\mu^{2}}\sum_{\mathbf{k},\sigma}c^{\dagger}_{\mathbf{k}\sigma}(c_{\mathbf{k}\sigma}c^{\dagger}_{\mathbf{k}\sigma}-1)c_{\mathbf{k}\sigma}+ \frac{1}{2V} \frac{4\pi e^{2}}{\mu^{2}}\sum_{\mathbf{k}\neq \mathbf{k}^{'}, \sigma\neq \sigma^{'}}c^{\dagger}_{\mathbf{k}\sigma}c_{\mathbf{k}\sigma}c^{\dagger}_{\mathbf{k}^{'}\sigma^{'}}c_{\mathbf{k}^{'}\sigma^{'}} \\
 & = - \frac{1}{2V} \frac{4\pi e^{2}}{\mu^{2}}\sum_{\mathbf{k},\sigma}c^{\dagger}_{\mathbf{k}\sigma}c_{\mathbf{k}\sigma}+ \frac{1}{2V} \frac{4\pi e^{2}}{\mu^{2}}\sum_{\mathbf{k}\sigma}c^{\dagger}_{\mathbf{k}\sigma} c_{\mathbf{k}\sigma}\sum_{\mathbf{k}^{'},\sigma^{'}}c^{\dagger}_{\mathbf{k}^{'}\sigma^{'}}c_{\mathbf{k}^{'}\sigma^{'}} \\
 & = \frac{1}{2V} \frac{4\pi e^{2}}{\mu^{2}}(-N+N^{2})
\end{align}$$
取$n= \frac{N}{V}$，注意到：
$$\begin{align}
H_{\text{b}} & = \frac{e^{2}}{2}\left( \frac{N}{V} \right)^{2} \int d^{3}x d^{3}x^{'} \frac{e^{-\mu|\mathbf{x}-\mathbf{x}^{'}|}}{|\mathbf{x}-\mathbf{x}^{'}| } \\
 & = \frac{1}{2} \frac{N^{2}}{V} \frac{4\pi e^{2}}{\mu^{2}}
\end{align}$$
$$\begin{align}
H_{\text{el-b}} & = -e^{2} \frac{N}{V} \sum_{i} \int d^{3}x \frac{e^{-\mu|\mathbf{x}-\mathbf{r}_{i}|}}{|\mathbf{x}-\mathbf{r}_{i}|} \\
 & = -e^{2} \frac{N^{2}}{V} \frac{4\pi}{\mu^{2}}
\end{align}$$
热力学极限下，只有$\mathcal{O}(N^{2})$项占据主导。得到：
$$V_{0}+H_{\text{b}}+H_{\text{el-b}}=0$$
故体系hamiltonian为：
$$H_{\text{el}}= \sum_{\mathbf{k},\sigma} \frac{k^{2}}{2m}c^{\dagger}_{\mathbf{k}\sigma}c_{\mathbf{k}\sigma}+ \frac{1}{2V} \sum_{\mathbf{k},\mathbf{k}^{'},\mathbf{q}\neq 0,\sigma,\sigma^{'}} \frac{4\pi e^{2}}{q^{2}}c^{\dagger}_{\mathbf{k}+\mathbf{q}\sigma}c^{\dagger}_{\mathbf{k}^{'}-\mathbf{q}\sigma^{'}}c_{\mathbf{k}{'\sigma^{'}}}c_{\mathbf{k}\sigma}$$

# 2. Wick定理的特殊形式

给定无相互作用系统hamiltonian $H=\sum_{ij}c^{\dagger}_{i}\epsilon_{ij}c_{j}$。对应单态基底$\{ \ket{\beta_{j}} \}$。由于无相互作用，hamiltonian一定可以对角化为$H=\sum_{i}a^{\dagger}_{i}h_{i}a_{i}$。对应单态基底$\{ \ket{\alpha_{j}} \}$。任取本征态$\ket{\Psi}=\sum_{j}a^{\dagger}_{j}\ket{0}$。

>[!Success] Proposition 2.1
>$$\langle a^{\dagger}_{i}a^{\dagger}_{j}a_{k}a_{l}\rangle  =  \langle a^{\dagger}_{i}a_{l}\rangle\langle a^{\dagger}_{j}a_{k}\rangle- \langle a^{\dagger}_{i}a_{k}\rangle\langle a^{\dagger}_{j}a_{l}\rangle
$$
## Proof.

我们有$\langle a^{\dagger}_{i}a^{\dagger}_{j}a_{k}a_{l}\rangle=\bra{a_{j}a_{i}\Psi}a_{k}a_{l}\Psi\rangle$。即去除两个粒子，看波函数是否重叠。显然仅有$(j,i)=(k,l)$或$(j,i)=(l,k)$时才有非零结果。先假设$i\neq j$。若$(j,i)=(k,l)$，那么：
$$\begin{align}
\langle a_{i}^{\dagger}a^{\dagger}_{j}a_{j}a_{i}\rangle & =-\langle a^{\dagger}_{i}a^{\dagger}_{j}a_{i}a_{j}\rangle= \langle a^{\dagger}_{i}a_{i}a^{\dagger}_{j}a_{j}\rangle \\
 & = \bra{\Psi} a^{\dagger}_{i}a_{i}a^{\dagger}_{j}a_{j}\ket{\Psi}  \\
 & = n_{i}n_{j}\bra{\Psi} \Psi\rangle \\
 & = n_{i}n_{j}  \\
 & = \langle a^{\dagger}_{i}a_{i}\rangle\langle a^{\dagger}_{j}a_{j}\rangle
\end{align}$$
若$(j,i)=(l,k)$，那么：
$$\begin{align}
\langle a_{i}^{\dagger}a^{\dagger}_{j}a_{i}a_{j}\rangle & = -\langle a^{\dagger}_{i}a_{i}a^{\dagger}_{j}a_{j}\rangle \\
 & = -n_{i}n_{j}  \\
 & = -\langle a^{\dagger}_{i}a_{i}\rangle \langle a^{\dagger}_{j}a_{j}\rangle
\end{align}$$
故：
$$\begin{align}
\langle a^{\dagger}_{i}a^{\dagger}_{j}a_{k}a_{l}\rangle & = \delta_{il} \delta_{jk} \langle a^{\dagger}_{i}a_{i}\rangle\langle a^{\dagger}_{j}a_{j}\rangle- \delta_{ik}\delta_{jl}\langle a^{\dagger}_{i}a_{i}\rangle\langle a^{\dagger}_{j}a_{j}\rangle
\end{align}$$
显然$\delta_{il}\langle a^{\dagger}_{i}a_{i}\rangle=\langle a^{\dagger}_{i}a_{l}\rangle$。这是因为$a^{\dagger}_{i}a_{l}\ket{\Psi}$显然与$\ket{\Psi}$垂直。对其它项分别改写得到：
$$\langle a^{\dagger}_{i}a^{\dagger}_{j}a_{k}a_{l}\rangle=\langle a^{\dagger}_{i}a_{l}\rangle \langle a^{\dagger}_{j}a_{k}\rangle-\langle a^{\dagger}_{i}a_{k}\rangle \langle a^{\dagger}_{j}a_{l}\rangle$$


若$i=j$，那么唯一非零情况为$i=j=k=l$：
$$\langle a^{\dagger}_{i}a^{\dagger}_{i}a_{i}a_{i}\rangle=\langle a^{\dagger}_{i}(1-a_{i}a^{\dagger}_{i})a_{i}\rangle=\langle a^{\dagger}_{i}a_{i}\rangle-\langle a^{\dagger}_{i}a_{i}a^{\dagger}_{i}a_{i}\rangle=n_{i}-n_{i}^{2}=0$$
这是因为费米子$n_{i}\in \{ 0,1 \}$。显然$i\neq j$得到的公式也适用。
>[!Right]
>$\blacksquare$

现在进行基变换$c^{\dagger}_{i}=\sum_{j}\bra{\alpha_{j}}\beta_{i}\rangle a^{\dagger}_{i},\ c^{}_{i}=\sum_{j}\bra{\beta_{i}}\alpha_{j}\rangle a^{}_{i}$。运用Einstein约定，我们有：
$$\begin{align}
\langle c^{\dagger}_{i}c^{\dagger}_{j}c_{k}c_{l}\rangle & =  \bra{\alpha_{i^{'}}}\beta_{i}\rangle \bra{\alpha_{j^{'}}} \beta_{j}\rangle \bra{\beta_{k}} \alpha_{k^{'}} \rangle \bra{\beta_{l}} \alpha_{l^{'}} \rangle \langle a^{\dagger}_{i^{'}}a^{\dagger}_{j^{'}}a_{k^{'}}a_{l^{'}}\rangle \\
 & = \bra{\alpha_{i^{'}}}\beta_{i}\rangle \bra{\alpha_{j^{'}}} \beta_{j}\rangle \bra{\beta_{k}} \alpha_{k^{'}} \rangle \bra{\beta_{l}} \alpha_{l^{'}} \rangle [\langle a^{\dagger}_{i}a_{l}\rangle\langle a^{\dagger}_{j}a_{k}\rangle- \langle a^{\dagger}_{i}a_{k}\rangle\langle a^{\dagger}_{j}a_{l}\rangle] \\
 & = \langle c^{\dagger}_{i}c_{l}\rangle \langle c^{\dagger}_{j}c_{k}\rangle- \langle c^{\dagger}_{i}c_{j}\rangle \langle c^{\dagger}_{j}c_{l}\rangle
\end{align}$$

>[!Success] Proposition 2.2
>$$\langle c^{\dagger}_{i}c^{\dagger}_{j}c_{k}c_{l}\rangle  = \langle c^{\dagger}_{i}c_{l}\rangle \langle c^{\dagger}_{j}c_{k}\rangle- \langle c^{\dagger}_{i}c_{j}\rangle \langle c^{\dagger}_{j}c_{l}\rangle$$

更广义的Wick定理为，如果中间的算子个数为奇数，直接得到零。这是好理解的。因为奇数个算符作用，永远不可能刚好回到原来的态$\ket{\Psi}$。如果中间的算子个数为偶数，则取一个上升算符，一个下降算符作为一对，先求这一对的平均值，将它们加上符号后求和。其中每一对的符号由对易关系交换算符引出的符号决定。注意配对的一对算子的相对顺序不能变。
## Ex:

例如$\langle a^{\dagger}_{i}a_{j}a^{\dagger}_{k}a_{l}\rangle= \langle a^{\dagger}_{i}a_{j}\rangle\langle a^{\dagger}_{k}a_{l}\rangle+ \langle a^{\dagger}_{i}a_{l}\rangle \langle a^{}_{j}a^{\dagger}_{k}\rangle$

# 3. 能量

将相互作用$H_{\text{int}}=\frac{1}{2V} \sum_{\mathbf{k},\mathbf{k}^{'},\mathbf{q}\neq 0,\sigma,\sigma^{'}} \frac{4\pi e^{2}}{q^{2}}c^{\dagger}_{\mathbf{k}+\mathbf{q}\sigma}c^{\dagger}_{\mathbf{k}^{'}-\mathbf{q}\sigma^{'}}c_{\mathbf{k}{'\sigma^{'}}}c_{\mathbf{k}\sigma}$当作微扰。令$\ket{F}$为费米气体基态，即完全填充费米球。我们先计算无微扰时的能量：
$$\begin{align}
E_{g}^{0} & = \left\langle  \sum_{\mathbf{k},\sigma} \frac{k^{2}}{2m}c^{\dagger}_{\mathbf{k},\sigma}c_{\mathbf{k},\sigma} \right\rangle \\
 & = \sum_{\mathbf{k},\sigma} \frac{k^{2}}{2m}\theta(k_{F}-k) \\
 & = 2 \int \frac{V}{(2\pi)^{3}} d^{3}k\frac{k^{2}}{2m}\theta(k_{F}-k) \\
 & = \frac{V}{2m\pi^{2}} \frac{k_{F}^{5}}{5}
\end{align}$$
我们计算一阶微扰：
$$\begin{align}
\langle c^{\dagger}_{\mathbf{k}+\mathbf{q},\sigma} c^{\dagger}_{\mathbf{k}^{'}-\mathbf{q},\sigma^{'} } c_{\mathbf{k}^{'},\sigma^{'}}c_{\mathbf{k},\sigma}\rangle & =  \langle c^{\dagger}_{\mathbf{k}+\mathbf{q},\sigma}c_{\mathbf{k},\sigma}\rangle\langle c^{\dagger}_{\mathbf{k}^{'}-\mathbf{q},\sigma^{'}}c_{\mathbf{k}^{'},\sigma^{'}}\rangle -\langle c^{\dagger}_{\mathbf{k}+\mathbf{q},\sigma}c_{\mathbf{k}^{'},\sigma^{'}}\rangle \langle c^{\dagger}_{\mathbf{k}^{'}-\mathbf{q},\sigma^{'}}c_{\mathbf{k},\sigma}\rangle \\
 & = \delta(\mathbf{q}=0) \theta(k_{F}-k) \theta(k_{F}-k)- \delta_{\sigma,\sigma^{'}}\delta(\mathbf{k}+\mathbf{q}=\mathbf{k}^{'}) \theta(k_{F}-|\mathbf{k}+\mathbf{q}|)\theta(k_{F}-k)
\end{align}$$
其中，$\theta(x)=\left\{\begin{aligned} 1,\ x\geq{0} \\ 0,\ x<0\end{aligned}\right.$ 为阶梯函数。阶梯函数是因为零温下占据数就是阶梯函数。第一项称为Hartree项，第二项称为Fock项。由于$\mathbf{q}\neq {0}$，所以只剩下Fock项。我们有：
$$\begin{align}
\langle H_{\text{int}}\rangle & = - \frac{1}{2V}  \sum_{\mathbf{k},\mathbf{k}^{'},\mathbf{q}\neq 0,\sigma} \frac{4\pi e^{2}}{q^{2}} \delta(\mathbf{k}+\mathbf{q}=\mathbf{k}^{'}) \theta(k_{F}-|\mathbf{k}+\mathbf{q}|)\theta(k_{F}-k)  \\
 & = - \frac{4\pi e^{2}}{V} \sum_{\mathbf{k}\neq \mathbf{k}^{'}}\theta(k_{F}-k^{'})\theta(k_{F}-k) \frac{1}{|\mathbf{k}-\mathbf{k}^{'}|^{2}} \\
 & = - \frac{4\pi e^{2}}{V} \int \frac{V^{2}}{(2\pi)^{6}}  d^{3}kd^{3}k^{'} \theta(k_{F}-k^{'})\theta(k_{F}-k) \frac{1}{|\mathbf{k}-\mathbf{k}^{'}|^{2}} \\
 & = - \frac{4\pi e^{2}V}{(2\pi)^{6}} \int d^{3}k^{' }\theta(k_{F}-k^{'}) \int_{0}^{k_{F}}dk\int_{0}^{\pi} 2\pi \sin \theta d\theta \frac{1}{k^{2}+k^{'2}-2kk^{'}\cos \theta} \\
 & = - \frac{4\pi e^{2}V}{(2\pi)^{6}}\int d^{3}k^{'}\theta(k_{F}-k^{'})\int_{0}^{k_{F}}k^{2} dk \frac{1}{-2k^{'}k} 2\pi \ln\left(  \frac{(k-k^{'})^{2}}{(k+k^{'})^{2}} \right) \\
 & = - \frac{4\pi e^{2} V}{(2\pi)^{6} } \int_{0}^{k_{F}}dk^{'} 4\pi k^{'2}  \frac{2\pi}{k^{'}}\int_{0}^{k_{F}}dkk (\ln|k+k^{'}|-\ln|k^{'}-k|) \\
 & = - \frac{4\pi e^{2}V}{(2\pi)^{6}}\int_{0}^{k_{F}}4\pi k^{'2}dk^{'} \frac{2\pi}{k^{'}}k_{F}k^{'}\left[ 1+ \frac{k_{F}^{2}-k^{'2}}{2k_{F}k^{'}}\ln \frac{|k^{'}+k_{F}|}{|k^{'}-k_{F}|} \right] \\
 & = - \frac{4\pi e^{2}V}{(2\pi)^{6}}8\pi^{2}k_{F}k_{F}^{3}   \int_{0}^{1}x^{2}dx\left[ 1+ \frac{1-x^{2}}{2x}\ln \frac{|1+x|}{|1-x|} \right]
\end{align}$$
可以计算这个积分为$\frac{1}{2}$。于是能量修正为：
$$\begin{align}
E_{g}^{1} & = \langle H_{\text{int}}\rangle \\
 & = - \frac{4\pi e^{2}V}{(2\pi)^{6}}8\pi^{2}k_{F}k_{F}^{3} \frac{1}{2} \\
 & = - \frac{3e^{2}}{4\pi}Nk_{F}
\end{align}$$
我们可以看到，这个能量修正由相同自旋的电子贡献，为负。也就是说，相同自旋电子倾向于排斥，降低库伦作用。

总能量为：
$$\begin{align}
E & = E^{0}_{g}+E^{1}_{g} \\
 & = \frac{V}{2m\pi^{2}} \frac{k_{F}^{5}}{5}- \frac{3e^{2}}{4\pi}Nk_{F}
\end{align}$$
令$r_{0}$为粒子间平均距离，满足$\frac{4}{3}\pi r_{0}^{3}= \frac{V}{N}$，$a_{0}= \frac{1}{me^{2}}$为Bohr半径，$r_{s}=\frac{r_{0}}{a_{0}}$。可以得到：
$$E= \frac{Ne^{2}}{2a_{0}}\left(  \frac{2.21}{r_{s}^{2}}- \frac{0.916}{r_{s}} \right)$$
