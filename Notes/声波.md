考虑理想气体。则存在Euler方程和质量守恒：
$$\begin{align}
 & \frac{\partial \rho}{\partial t}+ \nabla \cdot(\rho \mathbf{u})=0 \\
 & \rho \frac{\partial \mathbf{u}}{\partial t}+ \rho\mathbf{u}\cdot \nabla \mathbf{u}=-\nabla p
\end{align}$$
考虑在平衡态附近存在微扰$\rho=\rho_{0}+ \tilde{\rho},\ p=p_{0}+ \tilde{p}$。假设$\mathbf{u}$及其导数都很小。那么保留线性项，得到：
$$\begin{align}
 & \frac{\partial \tilde{\rho}}{\partial t}+\rho_{0}\nabla \cdot \mathbf{u}=0 \\
 & \rho_{0} \frac{\partial \mathbf{u}}{\partial t}=-\nabla \tilde{p}
\end{align}$$
对第一个等式求时间导，对第二个等式求散度，联立得到：
$$\frac{\partial^{2}\tilde{\rho}}{\partial t^{2}}- \nabla^{2}\tilde{p}=0$$
接下来需要联系$\tilde{\rho}$和$\tilde{p}$。假设热传导时间远大于气体运动时间，远大于气体微元的relaxation time。那么可以认为气体微元没有热传导，并时时处于平衡。那么在这个等熵过程中，对于任意体微元总有：
$$pV^{\gamma}=\text{const.}$$
除以质量的幂得到：
$$\frac{p}{\rho^{\gamma}}=\text{const.}$$
那么我们线性化：
$$\begin{align}
 & \frac{p_{0}+\tilde{p}}{(\rho_{0}+\tilde{\rho})^{\gamma} }=\text{const.} \\
\implies & (p_{0}+\tilde{p})\rho_{0}^{-\gamma}\left( 1- \gamma \frac{\tilde{\rho}}{\rho_{0}} \right)=\text{const.} \\
\implies & p_{0}\rho_{0}^{-\gamma}- \gamma \frac{p_{0}}{\rho_{0}} \tilde{\rho} \rho_{0}^{-\gamma}+ \tilde{p}\rho_{0}^{-\gamma} =\text{const.} \\
\implies &  p_{0}- \gamma \frac{p_{0}}{\rho_{0}}\tilde{\rho}+ \tilde{p}=\text{const.} \\
\implies &  \nabla^{2}\tilde{p}=\gamma \frac{p_{0}}{\rho_{0}}\nabla^{2}\tilde{\rho}
\end{align}$$
于是得到波动方程：
$$\frac{\partial^{2}\tilde{\rho}}{\partial t^{2}}- \gamma \frac{p_{0}}{\rho_{0}}\nabla^{2}\tilde{\rho}=0$$
