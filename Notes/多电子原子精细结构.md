# 1. Russel-Saunders coupling

考虑一个多电子原子，Hamiltonian为：
$$\begin{align}
H & = \sum_{i} \left(\frac{p_{i}^{2}}{2m}+V_{}(\mathbf{r}_{i})\right)+V_{\text{int}}(\{ \mathbf{r}_{i} \})
\end{align}$$
其中$i$为第$i$个电子的index。若每个电子存在本征态$\ket{\psi_{i}}$，则Hilbert空间由$\ket{\psi_{1}}\otimes \dots \otimes \ket{\psi_{N}}$张成。将径向，orbital，以及spin的态分离出来，我们进一步写：
$$\begin{align}
\ket{\psi_{1}} \otimes \dots \otimes \ket{\psi_{N}}  & = \ket{\{ n_{i} \}, \{ l_{i} \},\{ m_{l_{i}} \}} \otimes \ket{\{ s_{i} \},\{ m_{s_{i}} \}}  \\
 & = \ket{\{ n_{i} \},\{ l_{i} \}} \otimes \ket{\{ l_{i} \},\{ m_{l_{i}} \}} \otimes \ket{\{ s_{i} \},\{ m_{s_{i}} \}} 
\end{align}$$
我们可以将orbital的态互相耦合，spin的态也互相耦合。定义总角动量，总spin算符：
$$\begin{align}
\mathbf{L}=\sum_{i}\mathbf{l}_{i},\ \mathbf{S}=\sum_{i}\mathbf{s}_{i}
\end{align}$$
则可以进行基变换，使得基换为：
$$\begin{align}
\ket{\psi_{1}} \otimes \dots \otimes \ket{\psi_{N}}  & \leadsto \ket{\{ n_{i} \},\{ l_{i} \}}\otimes \ket{L,m_{L}} \otimes \ket{S,m_{S}}  
\end{align}$$
这称为Russel-Saunders coupling scheme。对于电子，实际上基底需要是反对称化的。基底是$\ket{\psi_{1}}\otimes\dots \otimes \ket{\psi_{N}}$的反对称的线性组合。即$\ket{\{ n_{i} \},\{ l_{i} \},L,S,m_{L},m_{S}}$的线性组合。

# 2. 多电子体系LS coupling项

现在考虑电子的LS coupling。我们有：
$$\begin{align}
H & = \sum_{i}\left( \frac{p_{i}^{2}}{2m}+V(\mathbf{r}_{i}) \right) +V_{\text{int}}(\{ \mathbf{r}_{i} \})+H_{\text{LS}} \\
 & = H_{0} +V_{\text{int}}+H_{\text{LS}}
\end{align}$$
我们可以得到LS coupling的hamiltonian：
$$\begin{align}
H_{\text{LS}} & = \sum_{i}A(\mathbf{r}_{i})\mathbf{l}_{i}\cdot \mathbf{s}_{i}\text{, where }A(\mathbf{r}_{i})= \frac{1}{2m^{2}c^{2}} \frac{1}{r_{i}} \frac{\partial V}{\partial r_{i}}
\end{align}$$
我们先来研究non-elementary angular momentum是怎么作为旋转生成元的。

>[!Success] Proposition 2.1
>Consider a system with N particles with angular momenta $l_{i},\mathbf{s}_{i}$. Then the generator of $SO(3)$ is $\mathbf{J}=\mathbf{L}+\mathbf{S}=\sum_{i}\mathbf{l}_{i}+\sum_{i}\mathbf{s}_{i}$.
## Proof.

设系统绕$\mathbf{n}$旋转$\theta$。则对于粒子$i$来说，其spatial和spin的态改变分别为：
$$\begin{align}
 & \ket{\phi_{i}} \mapsto \exp\left( - \frac{i}{\hbar}\mathbf{l}_{i}\cdot   \hat{\mathbf{n}}\theta  \right)\ket{\phi_{i}}  \\
 & \ket{\chi_{i}} \mapsto \exp\left( - \frac{i}{\hbar}\mathbf{s}_{i}\cdot  \hat{\mathbf{n}}\theta \right) \ket{\chi_{i}} 
\end{align}$$
则该粒子的态改变为：
$$\begin{align}
\ket{\psi_{i}} =\ket{\phi_{i}} \otimes \ket{\chi_{i}} &  \mapsto \exp\left( - \frac{i}{\hbar}\mathbf{l}_{i}\cdot  \hat{\mathbf{n}}\theta \right) \ket{\phi_{i}} \otimes \exp\left( - \frac{i}{\hbar}\mathbf{s}_{i}\cdot  \hat{\mathbf{n}}\theta \right) \ket{\chi_{i}}  \\
 & = \exp\left( - \frac{i}{\hbar}(\mathbf{l}_{i}+\mathbf{s}_{i})\cdot  \hat{\mathbf{n}}\theta \right)\ket{\phi_{i}} \otimes \ket{\chi_{i}}  \\
 & = \exp\left( - \frac{i}{\hbar}(\mathbf{l}_{i}+\mathbf{s}_{i})\cdot  \hat{\mathbf{n}}\theta \right)\ket{\psi_{i}}    
\end{align}$$
第一步到第二步用到了$[\mathbf{l}_{i},\mathbf{s}_{i}]=0$。然后总态的改变为：
$$\begin{align}
\ket{\psi} =\ket{\psi_{1}} \otimes \dots \otimes \ket{\psi_{N}}  & \mapsto \exp\left( - \frac{i}{\hbar}(\mathbf{l}_{1}+\mathbf{s}_{1}) \cdot  \hat{\mathbf{n}}\theta \right)\ket{\psi_{1}} \otimes\dots \otimes \exp\left( - \frac{i}{\hbar}(\mathbf{l}_{N}+ \mathbf{s}_{N})\cdot  \hat{\mathbf{n}}\theta \right)\ket{\psi_{N}}  \\
 & = \exp\left( - \frac{i}{\hbar}\sum_{i}(\mathbf{l}_{i}+\mathbf{s}_{i})\cdot  \hat{\mathbf{n}}\theta \right)\ket{\psi}  \\
 & = \exp\left( - \frac{i}{\hbar}\mathbf{J}\cdot  \hat{\mathbf{n}}\theta \right)\ket{\psi} 
\end{align}$$
其中，我用到了$[\mathbf{l}_{i}+\mathbf{s}_{i},\mathbf{l}_{j}+ \mathbf{s}_{j}]=0\text{ for }i\neq j$。
>[!Right]
>$\blacksquare$

接下来，由于$H_{0}+V_{\text{int}}$是标量算子，我们有：
$$\begin{align}
 & [H_{0}+V_{\text{int}},\mathbf{J} ]=0,\ [H_{0}+V_{\text{int}},\mathbf{L}+\mathbf{S}]=0
\end{align}$$
由于$\mathbf{S}$和$H_{0}+V_{\text{int}}$作用在不同空间上，我们还有：
$$[H_{0}+V_{\text{int}},\mathbf{S}]=0$$
故：
$$[H_{0}+V_{\text{int}},\mathbf{L}]=0$$






我们注意到：
$$\begin{align}
 & [\mathbf{l}_{i},\mathbf{L}]=0\implies[\mathbf{l}_{i},L^{2}]=0,\ [\mathbf{l}_{i},L_{z}]=0 \\
 & [\mathbf{s}_{i},\mathbf{S}]=0\implies [\mathbf{s}_{i},S^{2}]=0,\ [\mathbf{s}_{i},S_{z} ]=0
\end{align}$$
所以$\ket{L,m_{L}}$张成的直线是$\mathbf{l}_{i}$的不变子空间，$\ket{S,m_{S}}$张成的直线是$\mathbf{s}_{i}$的不变子空间。所以：
$$\begin{align}
\bra{\{ n_{i}^{'} \},\{ l_{i}^{'} \},L^{'},S^{'},m_{L}^{'},m_{S}^{'}} H_{\text{LS}}\ket{\{ n_{i} \},\{ l_{i} \},L,S,m_{L},m_{S}}  & = \sum_{i} \bra{n_{i}^{'},l_{i}^{'}} A\ket{n_{i},l_{i}} \bra{L^{'},m_{L}^{'}} \mathbf{l}_{i}\ket{L,m_{L}} \cdot \bra{S,m_{S}^{'}} \mathbf{s}_{i}\ket{S^{'},m_{S}^{}}  \\
 & = \delta_{L,L^{'}}\delta_{S,S^{'}}\sum_{i}\bra{n^{'}_{i}, l_{i}^{'} }A \ket{n_{i},l_{i}}  \bra{L,m_{L}^{'}} \mathbf{l}_{i}\ket{L,m_{L}} \cdot \bra{S,m_{S}^{'}} \mathbf{s}_{i}\ket{S,m_{S}} 
\end{align}$$
由projection theorem，我们知道：
$$\begin{align}
 & \bra{L^{},m_{L}^{'}} \mathbf{l}_{i}\ket{L,m_{L}}= \frac{\bra{L,m_{L} }\mathbf{l}_{i}\cdot \mathbf{L} \ket{L,m_{L}}   }{\hbar^{2}L(L+1)} \bra{L,m_{L}^{'}} \mathbf{L}\ket{L,m_{L}} \\
 & \bra{S,m_{S}^{'}} \mathbf{s}_{i}\ket{S,m_{S}} = \frac{\bra{S,m_{S}} \mathbf{s}_{i}\cdot \mathbf{S}\ket{\mathbf{S},m_{S}} }{\hbar^{2}S(S+1)} \bra{S,m_{S}^{'}} \mathbf{S}\ket{S,m_{S}}   
\end{align}$$
矩阵元可以写为：
$$\begin{align}
\bra{\dots} H_{\text{LS}}\ket{\dots} & =  \delta_{L,L^{'}}\delta_{S,S^{'}}\sum_{i}  \frac{\bra{n_{i}^{'},l_{i}^{'}} A\ket{n_{i},l_{i}} \langle \mathbf{l}_{i}\cdot \mathbf{L}\rangle_{L,m_{L}} \langle \mathbf{s}_{i}\cdot \mathbf{S}\rangle_{S,m_{S}} }{\hbar^{4}L(L+1)S(S+1)} \bra{L,S,m_{L}^{'},m_{S_{}}^{'}}\mathbf{L}\cdot \mathbf{S}\ket{L,S,m_{L},m_{S}} \\
  & =  \sum_{i} \frac{\bra{n_{i}^{'},l_{i}^{'}} A\ket{n_{i},l_{i}} \langle \mathbf{l}_{i}\cdot \mathbf{L}\rangle_{L,m_{L}} \langle \mathbf{s}_{i}\cdot \mathbf{S}\rangle_{S,m_{S}} }{\hbar^{4}L(L+1)S(S+1)}  \bra{L^{'},S^{'},m_{L}^{'},m_{S}^{'}} \mathbf{L}\cdot \mathbf{S}\ket{L,S,m_{L},m_{S}} 
\end{align}$$
