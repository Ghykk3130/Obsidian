
>[!Note] Definition 1
>Let $f:\mathbb{C}\rightarrow \mathbb{C}$ be a transformation. We say that $f$ is a conformal map $\zeta_{0}$ if given two curves $w_{1}(t),w_{2}(t)$ passing through $\zeta_{0}$, we have:
>$$\text{arg}\left(  \frac{\frac{d}{dt}(f \circ w_{1})\delta t}{\frac{d}{dt}(f \circ w_{2})\delta t} \right)= \text{arg}\left( \frac{\frac{d}{dt}w_{1}\delta t}{\frac{d}{dt}w_{2}\delta t} \right)$$

这即是说，任意两条曲线的切线在变换前后交角不变。我们下结论：

>[!Success] Proposition 1
>$f$ is a conformal map at $\zeta_{0}$ if $f$ is holomorphic at $\zeta_{0}$ and $f^{'}(\zeta_{0})\neq 0$.
## Proof.

任考虑两条在$t=0$过$\zeta_{0}$的曲线$w_{1}(t),w_{2}(t)$。在$\zeta_{0}$处切向量为：
$$\delta w_{1}= w_{1}^{'}(0)\delta t,\ \delta w_{2}=w_{2}^{'}(t)\delta t$$
得到交角$\text{arg}\left(  \frac{w_{1}^{'}(0)}{w_{2}^{'}(0)} \right)$。变换后的曲线为$\tilde{w}_{1}(t)=f(w_{1}(t)),\ \tilde{w}_{2}(t)=f(w_{2}(t))$。在$f(\zeta_{0})$处的切向量为：
$$\delta \tilde{w}_{1}= f^{'}(\zeta_{0}) w_{1}^{'}(0)\delta t,\ \delta \tilde{w}_{2}=f^{'}(\zeta_{0})w^{'}_{2}(0)\delta t$$
得到同样地交角$\text{arg}\left( \frac{w_{1}^{'}(0)}{w_{2}^{'}(0)} \right)$。
>[!Right]
>$\blacksquare$
