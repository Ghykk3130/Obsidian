# 1. Von-Neumann equation

回忆起经典统计系统。系统封闭等价于系统演化遵循Hamilton方程：
$$\dot{q}_{i}= \frac{\partial H}{\partial p_{i}},\   \dot{p}_{i}=- \frac{\partial H}{\partial q_{i}}$$
其中不存在damping term。那么容易证明：
$$\begin{align}
  \frac{D\rho(\mathbf{q},\mathbf{p},t)}{Dt} & = \frac{\partial \rho}{\partial t}+ \dot{q}_{i} \frac{\partial \rho}{\partial q_{i}}+  \dot{p}_{i} \frac{\partial \rho}{\partial p_{i}} \\
 & =  -\nabla \cdot(\mathbf{v}\rho)+  \dot{q}_{i} \frac{\partial \rho}{\partial q_{i}}+  \dot{p}_{i} \frac{\partial \rho}{\partial p_{i}} \\
 & = - \frac{\partial  \dot{q_{i}}}{\partial q_{i}} \rho- \frac{\partial  \dot{ p}_{i}}{\partial p_{i}}\rho- 
\dot{q}_{i} \frac{\partial \rho}{\partial q_{i}}-  \dot{p}_{i} \frac{\partial \rho}{\partial p_{i}}+\dot{q}_{i} \frac{\partial \rho}{\partial q_{i}}+  \dot{p}_{i} \frac{\partial \rho}{\partial p_{i}}  \\
 & = -\rho\left(  \frac{\partial^{2}H}{\partial q_{i}\partial p_{i}} - \frac{\partial^{2}H}{\partial p_{i}\partial q_{i}} \right) =0\end{align}$$
那么可以得到Liouville方程：
$$\begin{align}
 & \frac{\partial \rho}{\partial t}+  \dot{q}_{i} \frac{\partial \rho}{\partial q_{i}}+  \dot{p}_{i} \frac{\partial \rho}{\partial p_{i}}=0 \\
\implies & \frac{\partial \rho}{\partial t}=- \frac{\partial \rho}{\partial q_{i}} \frac{\partial H}{\partial p_{i}}+ \frac{\partial \rho}{\partial p_{i}} \frac{\partial H}{\partial q_{i}} \\
\implies &  \frac{\partial \rho}{\partial t}=-[\rho,H]_{\mathbf{q},\mathbf{p}}
\end{align}$$考虑一个量子统计系统。系统封闭等价于系统演化遵循Schrodinger方程：
$$i\hbar \frac{\partial}{\partial t}\ket{\psi} =H \ket{\psi} $$
那么可以得到von-Neumann方程：
$$\begin{align}
  \frac{\partial \rho}{\partial t}  & = \frac{\partial}{\partial t}(w_{i}\ket{\psi_{i}} \bra{\psi_{i}} ) \\
  & = w_{i} \left( \frac{\partial}{\partial t}\ket{\psi_{i}}  \right)\bra{\psi_{i}} + w_{I} \ket{\psi_{i}} \left(  \frac{\partial}{\partial t}\bra{\psi_{i}}  \right) \\
 & = \frac{1}{i\hbar}H\rho- \frac{1}{i\hbar}\rho H  \\
 & = - \frac{1}{i\hbar}[\rho,H]
\end{align}$$
# 2. Bloch vector

考虑二能级系统的density operator：
$$\rho=\begin{pmatrix}
\alpha & \beta \\
\beta ^{*} & 1-\alpha 
\end{pmatrix},\ \alpha\in \mathbb{R},\ \beta\in \mathbb{C}$$
存在三个自由度。那么可以重写：
$$\rho=\begin{pmatrix}
\frac{1+w}{2} & \frac{u-iv}{2} \\
\frac{u+iv}{2} & \frac{1-w}{2} 
\end{pmatrix}= \frac{1}{2}(1+\mathbf{B}\cdot \boldsymbol{\sigma}),\ \mathbf{B}=(u,v,w),\ u,v,w\in \mathbb{R}$$
定义$\mathbf{B}$为Bloch vector。我们可以得到Bloch矢的限制：

>[!Success] Proposition 2.1
>$$B<1\Leftrightarrow Tr(\rho^{2})<1,\ B=1\Leftrightarrow Tr(\rho^{2})=1$$
## Proof.

我们有：
$$Tr(\rho^{2})=\frac{1}{2}(u^{2}+v^{2}+w^{2})+ \frac{1}{2}$$
>[!Right]
>$\blacksquare$

所以可以认为Bloch矢分布在$S^{2}$包裹的区域。若$\mathbf{B}$在$S^{2}$上，pure。若$\mathbf{B}$在$S^{2}$包裹区域内部，impure。

当ensemble pure时，有$B=1$。容易证明$(u,v,w)$方向的自旋本征态为这个纯态。令$(u,v,w)=(\sin \theta \cos \phi,\sin \theta \sin \phi,\cos \theta)$。那么计算$\ket{\psi}=\begin{pmatrix}\cos  \frac{\theta}{2} \\ e^{i\phi}\sin \frac{\theta}{2} \end{pmatrix}$构成的密度矩阵：
$$\begin{align}
\begin{pmatrix}
\cos \frac{\theta}{2} \\
e^{i\phi}\sin \frac{\theta}{2} 
\end{pmatrix}
\end{align}\begin{pmatrix}
 \cos \frac{\theta}{2} & e^{-i\phi}\sin \frac{\theta}{2} 
\end{pmatrix}= \begin{pmatrix}
\frac{1+\cos \theta}{2} &  \frac{1}{2}\sin \theta  e^{-i\phi} \\
\frac{1}{2}\sin \theta e^{i\phi} &  \frac{1-\cos \theta}{2}
\end{pmatrix}= \begin{pmatrix}
\frac{1+w}{2} &  \frac{u-iv}{2} \\
\frac{u+iv}{2} & \frac{1-w}{2}
\end{pmatrix}$$
即可。

此外，容易发现$(u,v)\neq 0\Leftrightarrow\text{coherence}\neq 0$。

# 3. Interaction temperature

考虑考虑一个相互作用子系统构成的系统。我们假设在Schrodinger绘景下Hamiltonian可以写为如下形式：
$$H^{}_{tot}=H_{}+V$$其中$H$为只计算子系统内部动能和内部相互作用然后相加得到的Hamiltonian。$V$为各个子系统相互作用项之和。

令$\mathscr{U}$为$H$对应的时间演化算子，遵循：
$$i\hbar \frac{\partial}{\partial t}\mathscr{U}=H\mathscr{U}$$
令$\ket{\psi_{S}}$为$H^{}_{tot}$在Schrodinger绘景下的本征态。我们作变换：
$$\ket{\psi_{I}} =\mathscr{U}^{\dagger}\ket{\psi^{}_{S}} $$
我们令$X_{I}$为任意interaction picture下的算子。那么需要满足：
$$\begin{align}
 & \bra{\psi_{I}} X_{I}\ket{\psi_{I}} =\bra{\psi_{S}} X_{S}\ket{\psi_{S}}  \\
\implies & \bra{\psi_{S}} \mathscr{U}X_{I}\mathscr{U}^{\dagger}\ket{\psi_{S}} =\bra{\psi_{S}} X_{S}\ket{\psi_{S}}  \\
\implies & X_{I}=\mathscr{U}^{\dagger}X_{S}\mathscr{U}
\end{align}$$
>[! Definition] Definition 2.1
>To relate interaction picture and Schrodinger picture, we do:
>$$\ket{\psi_{I}} =\mathscr{U} \ket{\psi_{S}},\ X_{I}=\mathscr{U}^{\dagger}X_{S} \mathscr{U}^{}  $$
>where $\mathscr{U}$ is the time evolution induced by the Hamiltonian excluding the interaction term.

那么：
$$\begin{align}
i\hbar \frac{\partial}{\partial t}\ket{\psi_{I}}  & = i\hbar\left( \frac{\partial}{\partial t}\mathscr{U}^{\dagger} \right)\ket{\psi_{S}} + i\hbar \mathscr{U}^{\dagger} \left( \frac{\partial}{\partial t}\ket{\psi_{S}}  \right) \\
 & = -\mathscr{U}^{\dagger}H\ket{\psi_{S}} +\mathscr{U}^{\dagger}H^{}_{tot}\ket{\psi_{S}}  \\
 & = \mathscr{U}^{\dagger}V\ket{\psi_{S}}  \\
 & = \mathscr{U}^{\dagger}V \mathscr{U }\ket{\psi_{I}} \\
 & = V_{I}\ket{\psi_{I}} 
\end{align}$$

>[!Success] Proposition 2.1
>The Schrodinger's equation under interaction picture is:
>$$i\hbar \frac{\partial}{\partial t}\ket{\psi_{I}} =V_{I}\ket{\psi_{I}} $$
>where $V_{I}$ is the interaction Hamiltonian.

我们可以得到相互作用绘景下的von-Neumann方程。令$\rho_{I}=\mathscr{U}^{\dagger}\rho_{S}\mathscr{U}$。我们有：
$$\begin{align}
  \frac{\partial}{\partial t}\rho_{I} & = \left( \frac{\partial}{\partial t}\mathscr{U}^{\dagger} \right)\rho_{S}\mathscr{U}+  \mathscr{U}^{\dagger}\left( \frac{\partial \rho_{S}}{\partial t} \right)\mathscr{U}+  \mathscr{U}^{\dagger} \rho_{S} \left( \frac{\partial}{\partial t}\mathscr{U} \right) \\
 & = - \frac{1}{i\hbar} \mathscr{U}^{\dagger}H\rho_{S}\mathscr{U}- \frac{1}{i\hbar} \mathscr{U}^{\dagger}[\rho_{S},H+V]\mathscr{U}+ \frac{1}{i\hbar} \mathscr{U}^{\dagger}\rho_{S}H\mathscr{U} \\
 & =   - \frac{1}{i\hbar}\mathscr{U}^{\dagger}H\rho_{S}\mathscr{U}- \frac{1}{i\hbar}\mathscr{U}^{\dagger}[\rho_{S}(H+V)-(H+V)\rho_{S}]\mathscr{U}+ \frac{1}{i\hbar}\mathscr{U}^{\dagger}\rho_{S}H\mathscr{U} \\
 & = - \frac{1}{i\hbar}[\mathscr{U}^{\dagger}V\rho_{S}\mathscr{U}-\mathscr{U}^{\dagger}\rho_{S}V\mathscr{U}] \\
 & = - \frac{1}{i\hbar}[\mathscr{U}^{\dagger}V\mathscr{U}\mathscr{U}^{\dagger}\rho_{S}\mathscr{U}- \mathscr{U}^{\dagger}\rho_{S}\mathscr{U}\mathscr{U}^{\dagger}V\mathscr{U}] \\
 & = - \frac{1}{i\hbar}[\rho_{I},V_{I}]
\end{align}$$

>[!Success] Proposition 2.2
>The von-Neumann equation under interaction picture is:
>$$\frac{\partial \rho_{I}}{\partial t}=- \frac{1}{i\hbar}[\rho_{I},V_{I}]$$
>where $\rho_{I}=\mathscr{U}^{\dagger}\rho_{S}\mathscr{U}$.

# 3. 二能级开放系统

考虑一个开放量子系统。则$H^{}_{tot}=H_{}+V$。那么，本征态可以看做是$H_{}$本征态加上来自环境的微扰$V$，所以肯定不遵循$H_{}$写出的Schrodinger方程。那么系统密度算子不一定遵循$H$引出的von-Neumann方程。

我们假设密度算子遵循这样的方程：
$$\frac{\partial \rho}{\partial t}=- \frac{1}{i\hbar}[\rho,H]+L(\rho,\{ \ket{n}  \})$$
其中$\{ \ket{n} \}$为$H$本征态。称$L(\rho,\ket{n})$为Lindbladian。

变换到interaction picture。容易证明：
$$\begin{align}
  \frac{\partial}{\partial t}\rho_{I} & = \left( \frac{\partial}{\partial t}\mathscr{U}^{\dagger} \right)\rho_{S}\mathscr{U}+  \mathscr{U}^{\dagger}\left( \frac{\partial \rho_{S}}{\partial t} \right)\mathscr{U}+  \mathscr{U}^{\dagger} \rho_{S} \left( \frac{\partial}{\partial t}\mathscr{U} \right) \\
 & = - \frac{1}{i\hbar} \mathscr{U}^{\dagger}H\rho_{S}\mathscr{U}- \frac{1}{i\hbar} \mathscr{U}^{\dagger}[\rho_{S},H+V]\mathscr{U}+\mathscr{U}^{\dagger}L_{S}\mathscr{U}+ \frac{1}{i\hbar} \mathscr{U}^{\dagger}\rho_{S}H\mathscr{U} \\
 & =   - \frac{1}{i\hbar}\mathscr{U}^{\dagger}H\rho_{S}\mathscr{U}- \frac{1}{i\hbar}\mathscr{U}^{\dagger}[\rho_{S}(H+V)-(H+V)\rho_{S}]\mathscr{U}+ \frac{1}{i\hbar}\mathscr{U}^{\dagger}\rho_{S}H\mathscr{U} +L_{I}\\
 & = - \frac{1}{i\hbar}[\mathscr{U}^{\dagger}V\rho_{S}\mathscr{U}-\mathscr{U}^{\dagger}\rho_{S}V\mathscr{U}]+L_{I} \\
 & = - \frac{1}{i\hbar}[\mathscr{U}^{\dagger}V\mathscr{U}\mathscr{U}^{\dagger}\rho_{S}\mathscr{U}- \mathscr{U}^{\dagger}\rho_{S}\mathscr{U}\mathscr{U}^{\dagger}V\mathscr{U}] +L_{I}\\
 & = - \frac{1}{i\hbar}[\rho_{I},V_{I}]+L_{I}
\end{align}$$

>[!Success] Proposition 3.1
>In the interaction picture, we have:
>$$\frac{\partial}{\partial t}\rho_{I}=- \frac{1}{i\hbar}[\rho_{I},V_{I}]+L_{I}$$
>where $L_{I}=\mathscr{U}^{\dagger}L_{S}\mathscr{U}$.

一般来说，$L=L(\rho,\ket{n})$为$\rho,\{ \ket{n} \}$的多项式。由于$\mathscr{U}$的unitary性，容易得到：
$$L_{I}=L_{S}(\rho_{I},\{ \ket{n_{I}}  \})$$
## Ex: decay

对于n-level system，我们宣称存在这样一个Lindbladian：
$$\begin{align}
L & = - \frac{1}{2}\sum_{m}C_{m}^{\dagger}C_{m}\rho + \text{h.c.}+\sum_{m}C_{m}\rho C_{m}^{\dagger} \text{, where }C_{m}=\sqrt{ \Gamma_{m} }\ket{g_{m}} \bra{e_{m}}  
\end{align}$$
其中$m$为decay channel的index。$\ket{g_{m}},\ket{e_{m}}$为decay channel $m$的ground和excited state。

考虑二能级系统$span\{ \ket{g},\ket{e} \}$。我们考虑相互作用绘景，省略下标。则只存在一个decay channel $\ket{e}\rightarrow \ket{g}$。令第一个基为$\ket{g}$，第二个基为$\ket{e}$。那么：
$$C_{m}= \begin{pmatrix}
0 & \sqrt{ \Gamma } \\
0 & 0
\end{pmatrix}$$
容易得到：
$$L(\rho)=\begin{pmatrix}
\Gamma\rho_{e e} & - \frac{1}{2}\Gamma \rho_{ge} \\
- \frac{1}{2}\Gamma \rho_{eg} & -\Gamma \rho_{e e}
\end{pmatrix}$$
Assume free decay, 我们有$V_{S}=0$。那么$V_{I}=0$。所以：
$$\frac{\partial \rho}{\partial t}=L(\rho)$$
容易解得：
$$\begin{align}
 & \rho_{e e}(t)=\rho_{e e}(t=0)e^{-\Gamma t} \\
 & \rho_{gg}(t)=1-\rho_{ e e }(t) \\
 & \rho_{ge}(t)=\rho_{ge}(t=0)e^{- \frac{\Gamma}{2}t} \\
 & \rho_{eg}(t)=\rho_{ge}^{*}(t)
\end{align}$$
