# 1. Von-Neumann equation

回忆起经典统计系统。系统封闭等价于系统演化遵循Hamilton方程：
$$\dot{q}_{i}= \frac{\partial H}{\partial p_{i}},\   \dot{p}_{i}=- \frac{\partial H}{\partial q_{i}}$$
其中不存在damping term。那么容易证明：
$$\begin{align}
  \frac{D\rho(\mathbf{q},\mathbf{p},t)}{Dt} & = \frac{\partial \rho}{\partial t}+ \dot{q}_{i} \frac{\partial \rho}{\partial q_{i}}+  \dot{p}_{i} \frac{\partial \rho}{\partial p_{i}} \\
 & =  -\nabla \cdot(\mathbf{v}\rho)+  \dot{q}_{i} \frac{\partial \rho}{\partial q_{i}}+  \dot{p}_{i} \frac{\partial \rho}{\partial p_{i}} \\
 & = - \frac{\partial  \dot{q_{i}}}{\partial q_{i}} \rho- \frac{\partial  \dot{ p}_{i}}{\partial p_{i}}\rho- 
\dot{q}_{i} \frac{\partial \rho}{\partial q_{i}}-  \dot{p}_{i} \frac{\partial \rho}{\partial p_{i}}+\dot{q}_{i} \frac{\partial \rho}{\partial q_{i}}+  \dot{p}_{i} \frac{\partial \rho}{\partial p_{i}}  \\
 & = -\rho\left(  \frac{\partial^{2}H}{\partial q_{i}\partial p_{i}} - \frac{\partial^{2}H}{\partial p_{i}\partial q_{i}} \right) =0\end{align}$$
那么可以得到Liouville方程：
$$\begin{align}
 & \frac{\partial \rho}{\partial t}+  \dot{q}_{i} \frac{\partial \rho}{\partial q_{i}}+  \dot{p}_{i} \frac{\partial \rho}{\partial p_{i}}=0 \\
\implies & \frac{\partial \rho}{\partial t}=- \frac{\partial \rho}{\partial q_{i}} \frac{\partial H}{\partial p_{i}}+ \frac{\partial \rho}{\partial p_{i}} \frac{\partial H}{\partial q_{i}} \\
\implies &  \frac{\partial \rho}{\partial t}=-[\rho,H]_{\mathbf{q},\mathbf{p}}
\end{align}$$考虑一个量子统计系统。系统封闭等价于系统演化遵循Schrodinger方程：
$$i\hbar \frac{\partial}{\partial t}\ket{\psi} =H \ket{\psi} $$
那么可以得到von-Neumann方程：
$$\begin{align}
  \frac{\partial \rho}{\partial t}  & = \frac{\partial}{\partial t}(w_{i}\ket{\psi_{i}} \bra{\psi_{i}} ) \\
  & = w_{i} \left( \frac{\partial}{\partial t}\ket{\psi_{i}}  \right)\bra{\psi_{i}} + w_{I} \ket{\psi_{i}} \left(  \frac{\partial}{\partial t}\bra{\psi_{i}}  \right) \\
 & = \frac{1}{i\hbar}H\rho- \frac{1}{i\hbar}\rho H  \\
 & = - \frac{1}{i\hbar}[\rho,H]
\end{align}$$
