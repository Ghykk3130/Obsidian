考虑hamiltonian为$H$，不含时。系统处于平衡态时，有密度算子$\rho=\frac{e^{-\beta H}}{Z}$。现考虑$V(t)$作为微扰，从$t=-\infty$开启。于是hamiltonian为$H+V(t)$。系统不再处于平衡状态。我们想求任意算子$\mathcal{O}$的平均值。那么我们需要先求密度算子。

考虑相互作用绘景$\mathcal{O}_{I}=e^{ \frac{i}{\hbar}Ht}\mathcal{O}_{S}e^{- \frac{i}{\hbar}Ht}$。我们知道，在Schrodinger绘景中，密度算子满足von-Neumann方程（此时hamiltonian是$H+V$。）：
$$\frac{d\rho_{S}}{dt}= \frac{1}{i\hbar}[H+V,\rho_{S}]$$
容易证明，在相互作用绘景中：
$$\begin{align}
\frac{d\rho_{I}}{dt} & = \frac{i}{\hbar}H\rho_{I}+ e^{\frac{i}{\hbar}Ht} \frac{1}{i\hbar}[H+V,\rho_{S}]e^{- \frac{i}{\hbar}Ht} - \frac{i}{\hbar}\rho_{I}H \\
 & = -\frac{1}{i\hbar}[H,\rho_{I}]+ \frac{1}{i\hbar}[H,\rho_{I}]+ \frac{1}{i\hbar}\left[ e^{\frac{i}{\hbar}Ht}Ve^{- \frac{i}{\hbar}Ht}, e^{ \frac{i}{\hbar}Ht}\rho_{S}e^{- \frac{i}{\hbar}Ht}  \right] \\
 & = \frac{1}{i\hbar}[V_{I},\rho_{I}]
\end{align}$$
我们积分得到自洽方程：
$$\begin{align}
\rho_{I}(t) & = \rho_{I}(-\infty)+ \int_{-\infty}^{t}dt^{'} \frac{1}{i\hbar}[V_{I}(t^{'}),\rho_{I}(t^{'})] \\
 & = e^{ \frac{i}{\hbar}Ht} \frac{e^{-\beta H}}{Z}e^{- \frac{i}{\hbar}Ht}+ \frac{1}{i\hbar} \int_{-\infty}^{t}dt^{'}[V_{I}(t^{'}),\rho_{I}(t^{'})] \\
 & = \rho + \frac{1}{i\hbar} \int_{-\infty}^{t}dt^{'}[V_{I}(t^{'}),\rho_{I}(t^{'})]
\end{align}$$
我们可以用类似Dyson级数的方法，求迭代解。若微扰是小量，可以保留到一阶项。那么自然有：
$$\begin{align}
\rho_{I}(t) & = \rho+ \frac{1}{i\hbar}\int_{-\infty }^{t}dt^{'}[V_{I}(t^{'}),\rho]
\end{align}$$
那么算子$\mathcal{O}_{I}$的平均值为：
$$\begin{align}
\text{Tr}(\mathcal{O}_{I}(t)\rho_{I}(t)) & = \text{Tr}(\mathcal{O}_{I}(t)\rho)+ \frac{1}{i\hbar}\int_{-\infty}^{t}dt^{'}\text{Tr}(\mathcal{O}_{I}(t)[V_{I}(t^{'}),\rho])
\end{align}$$
处理左手边：
$$\text{Tr}(\mathcal{O}_{I}\rho_{I})= \text{Tr}\left( e^{\frac{i}{\hbar}Ht}\mathcal{O}_{S}\rho_{S}e^{- \frac{i}{\hbar}Ht} \right)= \text{Tr}\left( \mathcal{O}_{S}\rho_{S}e^{- \frac{i}{\hbar}Ht}e^{\frac{i}{\hbar}Ht} \right)=\langle \mathcal{O}(t)\rangle$$
处理第一项：
$$\begin{align}
\text{Tr}(\mathcal{O}_{I}(t)\rho ) & = \text{Tr}\left( e^{ \frac{i}{\hbar}Ht}\mathcal{O}_{S} e^{- \frac{i}{\hbar}Ht } \frac{e^{- \beta H}}{Z}  \right) \\
 & = \text{Tr}\left( \mathcal{O}_{S}e^{- \frac{i}{\hbar}Ht} \frac{e^{-\beta H}}{Z}e^{ \frac{i}{\hbar}Ht} \right) \\
 & = \text{Tr}\left( \mathcal{O}_{S} \frac{e^{-\beta H}}{Z} \right) =\text{Tr}(\mathcal{O}\rho)=\text{Tr}(\rho\mathcal{O})
\end{align}$$
处理第二项：
$$\begin{align}
\text{Tr}(\mathcal{O}_{I}[V_{I},\rho]) & = \text{Tr}(\mathcal{O}_{I}V_{I}\rho)-\text{Tr}(\mathcal{O}_{I}\rho V_{I}) \\
 & = \text{Tr}(\rho\mathcal{O}_{I}V_{I})-\text{Tr}(\rho V_{I}\mathcal{O}_{I}) \\
 & = \langle [\mathcal{O}_{I},V_{I}]\rangle
\end{align}$$
我们注意到，$\mathcal{O}_{I}=e^{\frac{i}{\hbar}Ht}\mathcal{O}_{S}e^{- \frac{i}{\hbar}Ht}=\mathcal{O}_{H}$就是按照$H$演化的Schrodinger绘景的算子。所以有：
$$\begin{align}
\boxed{\langle \mathcal{O}(t)\rangle= \text{Tr}(\rho\mathcal{O})+ \frac{1}{i\hbar }\int_{-\infty}^{t}dt^{'}\langle [\mathcal{O}_{I}(t),V_{I}(t^{'})]\rangle}
\end{align}$$
$\langle \delta \mathcal{O}(t)\rangle= \langle \mathcal{O}(t)\rangle-\text{Tr}(\rho\mathcal{O})$即$t$时刻物理量$\mathcal{O}$距离平衡的偏移。

若$V_{I}(t)=\mathcal{O}f(t)$，那么偏移可以写为：
$$\begin{align}
\langle \delta \mathcal{O}(t)\rangle & = \frac{1}{i\hbar}\int dt^{'}f(t^{'}) \langle [\mathcal{O}_{I}(t),\mathcal{O}_{I}(t^{'})]\rangle \\
 & = \int dt^{'}\chi(t,t^{'})f(t^{'})
\end{align}$$
将$\langle [\mathcal{O}_{I}(t),\mathcal{O}_{I}(t^{'})]\rangle$称为关联函数。我们得到Kubo公式：

>[!Success] Proposition 1 (Kubo's formula)
>Let the perturbation of the system be coupled with the observable $\mathcal{O}$ by $V(t)=\mathcal{O}f(t)$. Then the deviation from equilibrium is given by:
>$$\langle \delta \mathcal{O}(t)\rangle= \int_{-\infty}^{t} dt^{'}\chi(t,t^{'})f(t^{'}),\ \chi(t,t^{'})= \frac{1}{i\hbar}\theta(t-t^{'})\langle [\mathcal{O}_{I}(t), \mathcal{O}_{I}(t^{'})]\rangle$$
>The average value is taken in the unperturbed ensemble.

这里添加step function $\theta$是为了表示$t\geq t^{'}$的因果性。我们可以证明，$\chi(t,t^{'})=\chi(t-t^{'})$。

>[!Success] Proposition 2
>$\chi(t,t^{'})=\chi(t-t^{'})$
## Proof.

令$t\geq t^{'}$。任取$\tau\in \mathbb{R}$，我们有：
$$\begin{align}
\chi(t+\tau,t^{'}+\tau) & = \langle [\mathcal{O}_{I}(t+\tau), \mathcal{O}_{I}(t^{'}+\tau)] \rangle \\
 & = \text{Tr}(\rho_{}[\mathcal{O}_{I}(t+\tau), \mathcal{O}_{I}(t^{'}+\tau)]) \\
 & = \text{Tr}\left( \rho_{}e^{ \frac{i}{\hbar}H(t+\tau)}[\mathcal{O}_{I}(t), \mathcal{O}_{I}(t^{'})]e^{- \frac{i}{\hbar}H(t+\tau)} \right) \\
 & = \text{Tr}\left( e^{ \frac{i}{\hbar}H(t+\tau)}\rho[\mathcal{O}_{I}(t), \mathcal{O}_{I}(t^{'})]e^{- \frac{i}{\hbar}H(t+\tau)} \right)  \\
 & = \text{Tr}(\rho[\mathcal{O}_{I}(t),\mathcal{O}_{I}(t^{'})]) \\
 & = \chi(t,t^{'})
\end{align}$$
那么必有$\chi(t,t^{'})=\chi(t-t^{'})$。
>[!Right]
>$\blacksquare$

取Fourier变换$\chi(t-t^{'})= \frac{1}{2\pi}\int d\omega \chi(\omega)e^{i\omega (t-t^{'})}$，$f(t^{'})=\frac{1}{2\pi}\int d\omega^{'}f(\omega^{'})e^{i\omega^{'}t^{'}}$。于是便有：
$$\begin{align}
\langle \delta\mathcal{O}(t)\rangle & = \int_{-\infty}^{t}dt^{'} \frac{1}{2\pi}\int d\omega \chi(\omega)e^{i\omega(t-t^{'})}\int \frac{d\omega^{'}}{2\pi}f(\omega^{'})e^{i\omega^{'}t^{'}} \\
 & = \frac{1}{2\pi}\int d\omega d\omega^{'}\chi(\omega)e^{i\omega t}f(\omega^{'})\delta(\omega^{'}-\omega) \\
 & = \frac{1}{2\pi}\int d\omega \chi(\omega)f(\omega)e^{i\omega t}
\end{align}$$
所以显然：
$$\boxed{\langle \delta\mathcal{O}(\omega)\rangle=\chi(\omega)f(\omega)}$$
