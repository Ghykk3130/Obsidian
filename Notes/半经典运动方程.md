# 1. k-space中的hamiltonian

考虑Bloch电子。存在Hamilton方程：
$$H\ket{\psi_{n}(k)} =\epsilon_{n}\ket{\psi_{n}(k)} $$
我们可以考虑$H$在$\{ \ket{\psi_{n}(k)} \}$下的矩阵元：
$$\bra{\psi_{n^{'}}(k)} H\ket{\psi_{n}(k)}= \bra{u_{n^{'}}(k)} e^{-ikx}He^{ikx}\ket{u_{n}(k)} $$
如果定义k-space中的hamiltonian $H(k,x)=e^{-ikx}He^{ikx}$，那么矩阵元为：
$$\bra{u_{n^{'}}} H(k)\ket{u_{n}}=\bra{u_{n^{'}}^{}}e^{-ikx}He^{ikx}\ket{u_{n}} =\epsilon_{n}\bra{\psi_{n^{'}}} \psi_{n}\rangle=\epsilon_{n}\delta_{n,n^{'}}  $$本征方程变为：
$$H(k)\ket{u_{n}} =\epsilon_{n}\ket{u_{n}} $$
Explicit地写开hamiltonian：
$$\begin{align}
H(k) & = e^{-ikx}\left( \frac{p^{2}}{2m}+V(x) \right)e^{ikx} \\
 & = -\frac{\hbar^{2}}{2m} e^{-ikx}\nabla^{2} e^{ikx}+V(x) \\
 & = - \frac{\hbar^{2}}{2m}(\nabla+ik)^{2}+V \\
 & = \frac{(-i\hbar\nabla+\hbar k)^{2}}{2m}+V \\
 & = \frac{(p+\hbar k)^{2}}{2m}+V
\end{align}$$

>[!Quote] 为什么我们写$H(k)$而不是$H(k,x)$？
>一般来说，我们将$p,x$当作算符，将$k$当作参数。习惯上来说只把hamiltonian当作参数的函数，写成$H(k)$，而不是$H(k,x)$。

类似地，我们可以对任意算符作变换$\mathcal{O}\leadsto e^{-ikx}\mathcal{O}e^{ikx}$得到k-space中算符，然后用$\ket{u_{n}}$计算矩阵元。
# 2. Anomalous velocity 

我们将速度算子定义为${v}=\frac{{p}}{m}$。那么：
$$\begin{align}
 & [x,H]=\left[ x, \frac{p^{2}}{2m} \right]= i\hbar \frac{{p}}{m}\implies v= \frac{1}{i\hbar}[x,H]
\end{align}$$
我们可以将它换到k-space中：
$$\begin{align}
e^{-ikx}ve^{ikx} & = \frac{1}{i\hbar}e^{-ikx}(xH-Hx)e^{ikx} \\
 & = \frac{1}{i\hbar}(xH(k)-H(k)x) \\
 & = \frac{1}{\hbar}\left(  \left(\frac{\partial}{\partial k }e^{-ikx}\right) H e^{ikx}+ e^{-ikx}H\left( \frac{\partial}{\partial k}e^{ikx} \right) \right) \\
 & = \frac{\partial H(k)}{\hbar\partial k}
\end{align}$$
我们先证明一个定理：

>[!Success] Theorem 2.1 (Feynman-Hellmann theorem)
>Let $H(\lambda)$ be a hamiltonian with eigenstates $\ket{n(\lambda)}$, eigen energy $E_{n}(\lambda)$. If the eigen subspaces are non-degenerate, then:
>$$\frac{\partial E_{n}}{\partial \lambda}=\bra{n}  \frac{\partial H}{\partial \lambda}\ket{n} $$

^e5de3e

## Proof.

我们有：
$$\begin{align}
 & \bra{n} H\ket{n} =E_{n} \\
\implies & \bra{ \frac{\partial}{\partial \lambda}n} H\ket{n} + \bra{n}  \frac{\partial H}{\partial \lambda}\ket{n} + \bra{n} H \ket{ \frac{\partial}{\partial \lambda}n} = \frac{\partial E_{n}}{\partial \lambda} \\
\implies & E_{n}\left( \bra{\frac{\partial}{\partial \lambda}n} n\rangle+ \bra{n}  \frac{\partial}{\partial \lambda}n\rangle \right)+\bra{n}  \frac{\partial H}{\partial \lambda}\ket{n} = \frac{\partial E_{n}}{\partial \lambda}
\end{align}$$
而：
$$\begin{align}
 & \bra{n} n\rangle=1 \\
\implies & \bra{ \frac{\partial}{\partial \lambda}n} n\rangle+ \bra{n}  \frac{\partial}{\partial \lambda}n\rangle=0
\end{align}$$
于是得证。
>[!Right]
>$\blacksquare$

现在考虑$H=H(x,t)$，绝热演化。假设系统在开始演化时处于$\ket{u_{n}}$。由[[Berry phase#^291c90|Berry phase proposition 1.2]]得到一阶修正后的态：
$$\ket{\psi} = \ket{u_{n}} - i\hbar \sum_{m\neq n} \frac{\ket{u_{m}} \bra{u_{m}} \partial_{t}u_{n}\rangle}{\epsilon_{n}-\epsilon_{m}}$$
我们忽略phase，因为计算平均值时phase是gauge free的。那么：
$$\begin{align}
\langle v \rangle & = \bra{\psi}  \frac{\partial H}{\hbar \partial k}\ket{\psi} + \left[ i\hbar \sum_{m\neq n} \frac{\bra{\partial_{t}u_{n}} u_{m}\rangle}{\epsilon_{n}-\epsilon_{m}} \bra{u_{m}} \frac{\partial H}{\hbar \partial k} \ket{u_{m}}+\text{h.c.}   \right]  
\end{align}$$
前一项显然为$\bra{\psi} \frac{\partial H}{\hbar \partial k}\ket{\psi}= \frac{\partial\epsilon_{n}}{\hbar \partial k}$。后一项为：
$$\begin{align}
i\hbar \sum_{m\neq n} \frac{\bra{\partial_{t}u_{n}} u_{m}\rangle}{\epsilon_{n}-\epsilon_{m}} \bra{u_{m} } \frac{\partial H}{\hbar \partial k}\ket{u_{n}}  & = i\hbar \sum_{m\neq n} \bra{\partial_{t}u_{n}} u_{m}\rangle \bra{u_{m}}  \partial_{k}u_{n}\rangle \\
 & = i \bra{\partial_{t}u_{n}}(1-\ket{u_{n} }\bra{u_{n}}  )\ket{\partial_{k}u_{n}} \\
 & = i \bra{\partial_{t}u_{n}} \partial_{k}u_{n}\rangle - i \bra{\partial_{t}u_{n}} u_{n}\rangle \bra{u_{n}} \partial_{k}u_{n}\rangle  
\end{align}$$
则这一项加上它的h.c.为：
$$\begin{align}
i\bra{\partial_{t}u_{n}} \partial_{k}u_{n}\rangle-i\bra{\partial_{t}u_{n}} u_{n}\rangle\bra{u_{n}} \partial_{k}u_{n}\rangle-i\bra{\partial_{k}u_{n}} \partial_{t}u_{n}\rangle +i \bra{u_{n}} \partial_{t}u_{n}\rangle\bra{\partial_{k}u_{n}} u_{n}\rangle
\end{align}$$
借助[[半经典运动方程#^e5de3e|半经典运动方程 theorem 2.1]]的思路，容易证明$\bra{\partial_{t}u_{n}}u_{n}\rangle=-\bra{u_{n}}\partial_{t}u_{n}\rangle,\ \bra{\partial_{k}u_{n}}u_{n}\rangle=-\bra{u_{n}}\partial_{k}u_{n}\rangle$。于是整体化简为：
$$\begin{align}
\langle v\rangle & = \frac{\partial\epsilon_{n}}{\hbar \partial k}+i (\bra{\partial_{t}u_{n}} \partial_{k}u_{n}\rangle- \bra{\partial_{k}u_{n}} \partial_{t}u_{n}\rangle) \\
 & = \frac{\partial\epsilon_{n}}{\hbar \partial k}+\Omega^{n}_{tk}
\end{align} $$
如果$k$是多维的，可以改写为：
$$v_{i}= \frac{\partial\epsilon_{n}}{\hbar \partial k_{i}} +\Omega^{n}_{t,k_{i}}$$

>[!Success] Proposition 2.1
>$v_{i}= \frac{\partial\epsilon_{n}}{\hbar \partial k_{i}}+ \Omega^{n}_{t,k_{i}}$

>[!Quote] 关于Berry curvature的两种写法
>我们有时写$\Omega_{x_{i},x_{j}}$，这代表着$i\left( \bra{ \frac{\partial}{\partial x_{i}}\psi} \frac{\partial}{\partial x_{j}}\psi\rangle- \bra{ \frac{\partial}{\partial x_{j}}\psi} \frac{\partial}{\partial x_{i}}\psi\rangle \right)$。有时我们写$\Omega_{x_{i}}$，这代表着系统是三维的，且$\Omega_{x_{i}}= i \left(  \bra{ \frac{\partial}{\partial x_{j}}\psi} \frac{\partial}{\partial x_{k}}\psi\rangle- \bra{ \frac{\partial}{\partial x_{k}}\psi} \frac{\partial}{\partial x_{j}}\psi\rangle \right)=i\epsilon_{ijk} \bra{ \frac{\partial}{\partial x_{j}}\psi} \frac{\partial}{\partial x_{k}}\psi\rangle$，其中$(i,j,k)$是$(1,2,3)$的偶排列。

# 3. 存在电场时的anomalous velocity

考虑三维系统加上常电场。若选择规范$\mathbf{E}=-\nabla \phi$，则$\phi$是坐标的线性函数。这将在hamiltonian中产生$e\phi$的势能，破坏平移对称性。

为了保留平移对称性，我们选取规范$\mathbf{E}=- \frac{\partial \mathbf{A}}{\partial t}$。那么hamiltonian改写为：
$$H(t)= \frac{|\mathbf{p}+|e|\mathbf{A(t)}|^{2}}{2m}+V$$
固定$t$，hamiltonian仍然保持晶体的离散平移对称性。所以仍然可以将本征态写为Bloch态$e^{i\mathbf{q}\cdot \mathbf{x}}\ket{u_{n,\mathbf{q}}}$。相应地，由于$\mathbf{p}$只是正则动量，$\mathbf{p}+|e|\mathbf{A}$是机械动量，速度算子改写为：
$$\begin{align}
\mathbf{v} & = \frac{\mathbf{p}+|e|\mathbf{A}}{m}= \frac{1}{i\hbar}[\mathbf{x},H]
\end{align}$$
容易得到：
$$\begin{align}
e^{-i\mathbf{q}\cdot \mathbf{x}} \mathbf{v} e^{i\mathbf{q}\cdot \mathbf{x}}= \frac{\partial H(\mathbf{q},t)}{\hbar \partial \mathbf{q}},\ H(\mathbf{q},t)= e^{-i\mathbf{q}\cdot \mathbf{x}} H(t)e^{i\mathbf{q}\cdot \mathbf{x}}
\end{align}$$
注意到：
$$H(\mathbf{q},t)=e^{-i\mathbf{q}\cdot \mathbf{x}}H(t)e^{i\mathbf{q}\cdot \mathbf{x}}= \frac{|\mathbf{p}+\hbar \mathbf{q}+|e|\mathbf{A}|^{2}}{2m}+V$$
由于$\hbar \mathbf{q}+|e|\mathbf{A}$作为一个整体进入hamiltonian，可以定义$\mathbf{k}= \mathbf{q}+ \frac{|e|}{\hbar}\mathbf{A}$，从而写$H(\mathbf{k})= e^{-i\mathbf{q}\cdot \mathbf{x}}H(t)e^{i\mathbf{q}\cdot \mathbf{x}}$。$\ket{u_{n,\mathbf{q}}}$仍然是本征矢，而本征能量显然只跟$\mathbf{k}$有关，写作$\epsilon_{n}(\mathbf{k})$。

那么：
$$\begin{align}
e^{-i\mathbf{q}\cdot \mathbf{x}}v_{i}e^{i\mathbf{q}\cdot \mathbf{x}}= \frac{\partial H(\mathbf{k})}{\hbar \partial q_{i}}= \frac{\partial H(\mathbf{k})}{\hbar \partial k_{i}} 
\end{align}$$
计算变换后的速度算子的期望值，和2一样，得到：
$$\langle v_{i}\rangle= \frac{\partial\epsilon_{n}(\mathbf{k})}{\hbar \partial k_{i}}+\Omega^{n}_{t,q_{i}}$$

注意到：
$$\begin{align}
 &  \frac{\partial}{\partial t}= \frac{\partial}{\partial k_{j}} \frac{\partial k_{j}}{\partial A_{l}} \frac{\partial A_{l}}{\partial t}= \frac{\partial}{\partial k_{j}}\delta_{jl} \frac{|e|}{\hbar} (-E_{l})=- \frac{|e|}{\hbar}E_{j} \frac{\partial}{\partial k_{j}} \\
 & \frac{\partial}{\partial q_{i}} = \frac{\partial}{\partial k_{j}} \frac{\partial k_{j}}{\partial q_{i}}= \frac{\partial}{\partial k_{j}}\delta_{ij}= \frac{\partial}{\partial k_{i}}
\end{align}$$
于是：
$$\begin{align}
\Omega^{n}_{t,q_{i}} & = i \bra{ \frac{\partial}{\partial t}u_{n,\mathbf{q}}}  \frac{\partial}{\partial q_{i}}u_{n,\mathbf{q}}\rangle- \text{c.c.} \\
 & = -i \frac{|e|}{\hbar}E_{j} \bra{ \frac{\partial}{\partial k_{j}}u_{n,\mathbf{q}}} \frac{\partial}{\partial k_{i}}u_{n,\mathbf{q}}\rangle-\text{c.c.}
\end{align}$$
而：
$$\begin{align}
(\mathbf{E}\times \boldsymbol{\Omega}^{n})_{i} & = \epsilon_{ijk}E_{j} \Omega^{n}_{k} \\
 & = \epsilon_{ijk}E_{j}\epsilon_{klm} i \bra{ \frac{\partial}{\partial k_{l}}u_{n,\mathbf{q}}}  \frac{\partial}{\partial k_{m}}u_{n,\mathbf{q}}\rangle \\
 & = (\delta_{il}\delta_{jm}-\delta_{ij}\delta_{lm}  )i E_{j} \bra{ \frac{\partial}{\partial k_{l}}u_{n,\mathbf{q}}}  \frac{\partial}{\partial k_{m}}u_{n,\mathbf{q}}\rangle \\
 & = i E_{j} \bra{ \frac{\partial}{\partial k_{i}}u_{n,\mathbf{q}}}  \frac{\partial}{\partial k_{j}}u_{n,\mathbf{q}}\rangle-\text{c.c.}
\end{align}$$
所以$\Omega^{n}_{t,q_{i}}=-(\mathbf{E}\times \Omega^{n})_{i}$。则半经典运动方程可以写为：
$$\mathbf{v}= \frac{\partial\epsilon_{n}}{\hbar \partial \mathbf{k}}- \frac{|e|}{\hbar}\mathbf{E}\times \boldsymbol{\Omega}^{n}$$
我们还可以证明$\dot{ \mathbf{q}}=0$。由于$[T_{\mathbf{R}},H(t)]=0,\ \forall t$，而$U(t,0)=\exp\left( - \frac{i}{\hbar}\int_{0}^{t}dt^{'}H(t^{'}) \right)$，则$[T_{\mathbf{R}},U(t,0)]=0$。一个$0$时刻的Bloch态$e^{i\mathbf{q}\cdot \mathbf{x}}\ket{u_{n,\mathbf{q}}}$处于平移算子本征值为$e^{i\mathbf{q}\cdot \mathbf{R}}$的本征子空间中。在$t$时刻，我们有：
$$T_{\mathbf{R}}U(t,0)e^{i\mathbf{q}\cdot \mathbf{x}}\ket{u_{n,\mathbf{q}}} =U(t,0)T_{\mathbf{R}}e^{i\mathbf{q}\cdot \mathbf{x}}\ket{u_{n,\mathbf{q}}} =e^{i\mathbf{q}\cdot \mathbf{R}}U(t,0)e^{i\mathbf{q}\cdot \mathbf{x}}\ket{u_{n,\mathbf{q}}} $$
于是演化后的态$U(t,0)e^{i\mathbf{q}\cdot \mathbf{x}}\ket{u_{n,\mathbf{q}}}$仍然处在相同的本征子空间中。所以$\mathbf{q}$是不变的。从此得到第二个运动方程：
$$\begin{align}
 & \dot{\mathbf{q}}= \dot{\mathbf{k}}- \frac{|e|}{\hbar} \frac{\partial \mathbf{A}}{\partial t}=0 \\
\implies & \hbar\dot{\mathbf{k}}=e\mathbf{E}
\end{align}$$

>[!Success] Proposition 3.1
>$$\mathbf{v}= \frac{\partial\epsilon_{n}(\mathbf{k})}{\hbar \partial \mathbf{k}}- \frac{|e|}{\hbar}\mathbf{E}\times \boldsymbol{\Omega}^{n},\ \hbar  \dot{\mathbf{k}}=e\mathbf{E}$$
