# 1. 输运积分

回忆起稳态下Boltzmann方程：
$$\begin{align}
 & \mathbf{v}\cdot \nabla f+ \frac{\mathbf{F}}{\hbar}\cdot \nabla_{\mathbf{k}} f=- \frac{f-f^{0}}{\tau(\epsilon)}
\end{align}$$
在线性化下，我们有：
$$\begin{align}
\nabla f & \approx \nabla f^{0} \\
 & = \nabla T \cdot \frac{\partial f^{0}}{\partial T}+ \nabla \mu \cdot \frac{\partial f^{0}}{\partial \mu} \\
 & = \nabla T \cdot \frac{\mu-\epsilon}{T} \frac{\partial f^{0}}{\partial\epsilon}- \nabla \mu \cdot \frac{\partial f^{0}}{\partial\epsilon} \\
 & = \left( - \frac{\partial f^{0}}{\partial\epsilon} \right)\left(  \frac{\epsilon-\mu}{T}\nabla T+\nabla \mu \right) 
\end{align}$$
$$\begin{align}
\nabla_{\mathbf{k}}f & \approx \nabla_{\mathbf{k}}f^{0} \\
 & =\frac{\partial\epsilon}{\partial \mathbf{k} }  \frac{\partial f^{0}}{\partial\epsilon }= \hbar \mathbf{v} \frac{\partial f^{0}}{\partial\epsilon}
\end{align}$$
我们解得平衡偏移量：
$$\begin{align}
g=f-f^{0} & =- \tau \mathbf{v}\cdot\left( -\frac{\partial f^{0}}{\partial\epsilon} \right)\left( \nabla \mu-\mathbf{F}+ \frac{\epsilon-\mu}{T}\nabla T \right)
\end{align}$$
如果是电子作为载流子，受到电场驱动，我们自然有：
$$g=-\tau \mathbf{v}\cdot\left( - \frac{\partial f^{0}}{\partial\epsilon} \right)\left( \nabla \mu+|e|\mathbf{E}+ \frac{\epsilon-\mu}{T}\nabla T \right)$$
计算电流密度：
$$\begin{align}
\mathbf{J}^{e} & =- |e| \int \frac{d^{3}k}{(2\pi)^{3}}\mathbf{v} g \\
 & = -|e|\int \frac{d^{3}k}{(2\pi)^{3}}\mathbf{v}\left[ \tau \mathbf{v}\cdot \left( - \frac{\partial f^{0}}{\partial\epsilon} \right)\left( \nabla \mu+|e|\mathbf{E}+ \frac{\epsilon-\mu}{T}\nabla T \right) \right]
\end{align}$$
假设各项同性，则积分可以化为：$\int d^{3}k v_{i}v_{j}\leadsto \int d^{3}k \frac{1}{3}v^{2}\delta_{ij}$。再换到能量空间$\int \frac{d^{3}k}{(2\pi)^{3}}\leadsto \int d\epsilon N(\epsilon)$。那么：
$$\begin{align}
\mathbf{J}^{e} & = e^{2} \int d\epsilon N(\epsilon) \left( - \frac{\partial f^{0}}{\partial\epsilon} \right) \frac{1}{3}v^{2}\tau\left( \mathbf{E}+ \frac{1}{|e|} \nabla \mu \right)+ \frac{|e|}{T}\int d\epsilon N(\epsilon)\left( - \frac{\partial f^{0}}{\partial\epsilon} \right) \frac{1}{3}v^{2}\tau(\epsilon-\mu)\nabla T
\end{align}$$

>[!Quote] 各向同性
>我们实际上遇到的积分形如$J^{e}_{i}=\int d^{3}k v_{i}v_{j}A_{j}$。假设色散关系是各向同性的，即：
>$$\epsilon=\epsilon(k)$$
>那么：
>$$v_{i}= \frac{1}{\hbar} \frac{\partial\epsilon}{\partial k_{i}}= \frac{1}{\hbar} \frac{\partial\epsilon}{\partial k} \frac{k_{i}}{k}$$
>注意到：
>$$\begin{align}
 v & = \sqrt{  \left( \frac{1}{\hbar} \frac{\partial\epsilon}{\partial k_{i}} \right) } \\
 & = \sqrt{ \left( \frac{1}{\hbar} \frac{\partial\epsilon}{\partial k} \frac{k_{i}}{k} \right) } \\
 & = \frac{1}{\hbar} \frac{\partial\epsilon}{\partial k}
\end{align}$$
>所以$v_{i}=v \frac{k_{i}}{k}$。于是：
>$$\begin{align}
J^{e}_{i} & = \int d^{3}k \frac{v^{2}}{k^{2}} k_{i}k_{j}A_{j} \\
 & = A_{j}\int k^{2}dk \frac{v^{2}}{k^{2}} \int d\Omega k_{i}k_{j}
\end{align}$$
>取$k_{x}=k\sin \theta \cos \phi,\ k_{y}=k\sin \theta \sin \phi$，我们可以计算：
>$$\begin{align}
\int d\Omega k_{x}k_{x} & = \frac{\pi^{2}k^{2}}{2},\ \int d\Omega k_{x}k_{y}=0
\end{align}$$
>由于这个结果应该独立于坐标的选择，所以$\int d\Omega k_{i}k_{j}=\delta_{ij} \frac{4\pi^{2}k^{2}}{3}$。那么：
>$$\begin{align}
J^{e}_{i} & = A_{j}\int dk v^{2}\delta_{ij} \frac{4\pi^{2}k^{2}}{3} \\
 & = A_{j} \int k^{2}dkd\Omega v^{2} \frac{1}{3}\delta_{ij} \\
 & = A_{j}\int d^{3}k \frac{1}{3}v^{2}\delta_{ij}
\end{align}$$

我们注意到两项都有统一的形式。定义输运积分：

>[!Note] Definition 1.1
>Define the transport integral:
>$$K_{n}= \int d\epsilon N(\epsilon)\left( - \frac{\partial f^{0}}{\partial\epsilon} \right) \frac{1}{3}v^{2}\tau(\epsilon-\mu)^{n}$$

那么电流可以写为：
$$\mathbf{J}^{e}=e^{2}K_{0}\left( \mathbf{E}+ \frac{1}{|e|} \nabla \mu \right)+ |e|K_{1} \frac{\nabla T}{T}=e^{2}K_{0}\mathbf{E}^{*}+|e| K_{1} \frac{\nabla T}{T}$$
定义有效电场为$\mathbf{E}^{*}=\mathbf{E}+ \frac{1}{|e|} \nabla \mu$。同理，我们可以计算热流：
$$\begin{align}
\mathbf{J}^{q} & = \mathbf{J}^{\epsilon}-\mu \mathbf{J}^{N} \\ & =
\int \frac{d^{3}k}{(2\pi)^{3} }(\epsilon-\mu)\mathbf{v}g \\
 & = -|e|\int d\epsilon N(\epsilon)\left( - \frac{\partial f^{0}}{\partial\epsilon} \right)(\epsilon-\mu) \frac{1}{3}v^{2}\tau(\mathbf{E}-\nabla \mu)+ \int d\epsilon N(\epsilon)\left( - \frac{\partial f^{0}}{\partial\epsilon} \right)(\epsilon-\mu)^{2} \frac{1}{3}v^{2}\tau \frac{\nabla T}{T} \\
 & = -|e| K_{1}\mathbf{E}^{*}- K_{2} \frac{\nabla T}{T}
\end{align}$$
那么：

>[!Success] Proposition 1
>$$\begin{align}
\begin{pmatrix}
\mathbf{J}^{e} \\
\mathbf{J}^{q}
\end{pmatrix}=\begin{pmatrix}
e^{2}K_{0}  & -|e|K_{1} \\
 -|e|K_{1} & K_{2}
\end{pmatrix} \begin{pmatrix}
\mathbf{E}^{*} \\
-\nabla T /T
\end{pmatrix}
\end{align}$$

我们定义输运系数：

>[!Note] Definition 1.2
>Define:
>- electrical conductivity: $\sigma= \frac{J^{e}}{E^{*}}$ when $\nabla T=0$
>- Peltier coefficient: $\alpha_{}=- \frac{J^{e}}{\nabla T}$ when $E^{*}=0$
>- Seebeck coefficient: $S_{}= \frac{E^{*}}{J^{q}}$ when $J_{}^{e}=0$
>- thermal conductivity: $\kappa_{}=- \frac{J^{q}}{E^{*}}$ when $J^{e}=0$
## Ex:

在无温度梯度时，可以得到电导率：
$$\sigma=e^{2}K_{0}$$
假设色散关系为$\epsilon= \frac{\hbar^{2}k^{2}}{2m^{*}}$。低温下，我们有：
$$\begin{align}
K_{0} & = \int_{-\infty}^{\infty}d\epsilon N(\epsilon) \left( - \frac{\partial f^{0}}{\partial\epsilon} \right) \frac{1}{3}v^{2}\tau \\
 & \approx N(\epsilon_{F}) \frac{1}{3}v^{2}(\mathbf{k}_{F})\tau
\end{align}$$
容易得到$N(\epsilon_{F})= \frac{4m^{*}}{h^{2}}k_{F}$， $v(\mathbf{k}_{F})= \frac{\hbar}{m^{*}}k_{F}$。那么：
$$\begin{align}
\sigma & = \frac{1}{3} \frac{e^{2}}{m^{*}\pi^{2}}\tau k_{F}^{3}
\end{align}$$
我们计算电子密度：
$$\begin{align}
n & = 2\frac{1}{V } \int_{k\leq k_{F}} \frac{V}{(2\pi)^{3}} d^{3}k \\
 & = \frac{1}{3} \frac{k_{F}^{3}}{\pi^{2}}
\end{align}$$
于是：
$$\sigma=n \frac{e^{2}}{m^{*}}\tau=n|e|\mu$$
这和Drude模型得出来的一样！

# 2. 输运系数之间的关系

若样本温度均匀，我们可以取$\nabla \mu=0$。此时得到电导率：
$$\sigma_{x x}= e^{2}K_{0}$$
若样本没有电流，则：
$$\begin{align}
  & 0= e^{2}K_{0}\mathbf{E}^{*}+|e|K_{1} \frac{\nabla T}{T} \\
\implies & \mathbf{E}^{*}= -\frac{1}{|e|} \frac{K_{1}}{K_{0}} \frac{\nabla T}{T}
\end{align}$$
我们解得热导率：
$$\begin{align}
\mathbf{J}^{q} & = \left( \frac{K_{1}^{2}}{K_{0}}-K_{2}\right) \frac{\nabla T}{T} \\
\implies \kappa_{} & = \frac{1}{T}\left( K_{2}- \frac{K_{1}^{2}}{K_{0}} \right)
\end{align}$$
令$\phi(\epsilon)=N(\epsilon) \frac{1}{3}v^{2}\tau$。在低温下，有Sommerfeld展开：
$$\begin{align}
K_{0} & =\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}d\epsilon \phi(\epsilon)\left( - \frac{\partial f^{0}}{\partial\epsilon} \right)   \approx \phi(\mu)\\
K_{1}  &  = \int_{-\infty}^{\infty}d\epsilon \phi(\epsilon)\left( - \frac{\partial f^{0}}{\partial\epsilon} \right)(\epsilon-\mu)\approx \frac{\pi^{2}}{3}(kT)^{2} \phi^{'}(\mu) \\
  K_{2} & =\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}d\epsilon \phi(\epsilon)\left( - \frac{\partial f^{0}}{\partial\epsilon} \right)(\epsilon-\mu)^{2}\approx \frac{\pi^{2}}{3}(kT)^{2} \phi(\mu) 
\end{align}$$

>[!Quote] Sommerfeld展开
>当$T\rightarrow 0$，则：
>$$\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}\delta\epsilon H(\epsilon)\left( - \frac{\partial f^{0}}{\partial\epsilon} \right)\approx H(\mu)+ \frac{\pi^{2}}{6}(kT)^{2}H^{''}(\mu)$$

由于$\frac{K_{1}^{2}}{K_{0}}\sim\mathcal{O}(T^{4}),\ K_{2}\sim\mathcal{O}(T^{2})$，所以低温下：
$$\kappa_{}\approx  \frac{1}{T}K_{2}\approx  \frac{\pi^{2}}{3}k^{2}T\phi(\mu)$$
而$\sigma_{}\approx e^{2}\phi(\mu)$。所以得到Wiedmann-Franz律：

>[!Success] Proposition 2.1
>$$\frac{\kappa_{}}{\sigma_{}T}= \frac{\pi^{2}}{3}\left( \frac{k}{e} \right)^{2}=L_{0}$$

这里不写角标，是因为容易类似地证明WF律对于横向component也成立。

我们还可以得到比热容。计算：
$$\begin{align}
c & = \frac{1}{V} \frac{\partial E}{\partial T} \\
 & = \frac{\partial}{\partial T} \int d\epsilon N(\epsilon)\epsilon f^{0} \\  & =  \int d\epsilon N\epsilon \frac{\partial f^{0}}{\partial T}+ \int d\epsilon N\epsilon \frac{\partial f^{0}}{\partial \mu} \frac{\partial \mu}{\partial T}\\

 & = \int d\epsilon N(\epsilon) \epsilon\left( - \frac{\partial f^{0}}{\partial\epsilon} \right)\left(  \frac{\epsilon-\mu}{T} + \frac{\partial \mu}{\partial T}\right) \\
   
\end{align}$$
定义$\mathscr{K}_{n}=\int d\epsilon N(\epsilon)\left( - \frac{\partial f^{0}}{\partial\epsilon} \right)(\epsilon-\mu)^{n}$。粒子数守恒约束态密度：
$$\begin{align}
 & \frac{\partial}{\partial T}\int d\epsilon N(\epsilon)f^{0}=0 \\
\implies & \int d\epsilon N \frac{\partial f^{0}}{\partial T}+ \int d\epsilon N \frac{\partial f^{0}}{\partial \mu} \frac{\partial \mu}{\partial T}=0 \\
\implies & \int d\epsilon N\left( - \frac{\partial f^{0}}{\partial\epsilon} \right)\left( \frac{\epsilon-\mu}{T}+ \frac{\partial \mu}{\partial T} \right)=0 \\
\implies & \frac{\mathscr{K}_{1}}{T}+ \frac{\partial \mu}{\partial T}\mathscr{K}_{0}=0
\end{align}$$
于是我们在$c$中减去$\int d\epsilon N\left( - \frac{\partial f^{0}}{\partial\epsilon} \right)\left( \frac{\epsilon-\mu}{T}+ \frac{\partial \mu}{\partial T} \right)=0$：
$$\begin{align}
c & = \int d\epsilon N(\epsilon-\mu)\left( - \frac{\partial f^{0}}{\partial\epsilon} \right)\left( \frac{\epsilon-\mu}{T}- \frac{\mathscr{K}_{1}}{T\mathscr{K}_{0}} \right)= \frac{1}{T}\left( \mathscr{K}_{2}- \frac{\mathscr{K}_{1}^{2}}{\mathscr{K}_{0}} \right)
\end{align}$$
在低温下只需要把$K_{n}$展开中的$\phi\leadsto N$。显然有Sommerfeld展开：
$$\begin{align}
\mathscr{K}_{0} & =\approx N(\mu)\\
\mathscr{K}_{1}  &  \approx \frac{\pi^{2}}{3}(kT)^{2} N^{'}(\mu) \\
  \mathscr{K}_{2} & \approx \frac{\pi^{2}}{3}(kT)^{2} N(\mu) 
\end{align}$$
忽略掉高阶$T$项，我们有$\kappa_{}\approx  \frac{1}{T} K_{2}$，$c\approx \frac{1}{T}\mathscr{K}_{2}$。于是：
$$\begin{align}
\kappa_{} & \approx \frac{1}{3}v^{2}\tau c
\end{align}$$
>[!Success] Proposition 2.2
>As $T\rightarrow 0$, we have $\kappa_{}= \frac{1}{3}v^{2}\tau c$

可将表达式写为$\kappa_{}= \frac{1}{3}v\cdot(v\tau)\cdot C$。即热导率为载流子速度，乘以平均自由程，再乘以载流子携带的热量，外加$1 /3$的系数。

我们可以推导Mott关系。限制$J^{e}=0$，我们得到Seebeck系数$S_{}= \frac{E_{}^{*}}{\nabla T}$。我们有：
$$\begin{align}
J^{e}=e^{2}K_{0}E^{*}+|e| K_{1} \frac{\nabla T}{T}=0\implies S_{}=- \frac{1}{|e|T} \frac{K_{1}}{K_{0}}
\end{align}$$
低温下，容易得到：
$$\begin{align}
S_{} & =- \frac{1}{|e|T} \frac{\pi^{2}}{3}(kT)^{2} \left.\frac{\phi^{'}}{\phi}\right|_{\epsilon=\mu} \\
 & = - \frac{1}{|e|} \frac{\pi^{2}}{3}k^{2}T \frac{\partial}{\partial\epsilon}(\ln \phi)
\end{align}$$
由$\sigma_{}=e^{2}K_{0}=e^{2}\phi(\mu)$得到：
$$S_{}=- \frac{1}{|e|} \frac{\pi^{2}}{3}k^{2}T \left. \frac{\partial}{\partial\epsilon}(\ln \sigma) \right|_{\epsilon=\mu}$$
>[!Success] Proposition 2.3 (Mott's relation)
>$$S_{}=- \frac{\pi^{2}k^{2}T}{3|e|}  \left. \frac{\partial}{\partial\epsilon}(\ln \sigma) \right|_{\epsilon=\mu}$$
